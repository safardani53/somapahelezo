Victoria's Secret Angels Spread Cheer in 12 Days of Christmas Video

Mistletoe, carols — and angels! The Victoria’s Secret Angels are kicking off the holidays with glitz, glam and plenty of lingerie! Us Weekly has the exclusive first look at the models spreading good cheer in their special “12 Days of Christmas” music video.

The merry (and bright!) clip features runway favorites such as Adriana Lima, Alessandra Ambrosio, Lily Aldridge, Candice Swanepoel, Behati Prinsloo, Elsa Hosk and Jasmine Tookes.

The ladies show off their singing chops in the animated clip, as each pops out from behind closed doors. They wear their famous wings, rock barely-there outfits, and have a few laughs at their own expense.

“Wait, do I need to close and open [the door] again?” Ambrosio asks in one outtake. 

Aldridge adds, “I’m confused as to where we are in the song!”

As for some of the 12 gifts the beauties are offering to cheery customers? VS gift cards, bras, silky robes, fragrances, naughty nighties and sexy secrets.
