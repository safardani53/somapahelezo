Taylor Swift Debuts &quot;Story of Us&quot; Music Video

Taylor Swift acts like a schoolgirl in her latest music video.

For “Story of Us,” Swift depicts herself as a college student going through a difficult relationship. She’s on record as saying the single and the video are both based on an awkward encounter with an ex.

“Anytime I’m onstage or watching the music video, the first thing that comes into my mind is the person I wrote the song about,” Taylor told MTV News. “It’s like the first thing that you think of is the moment that inspired the song, which is that excruciating, awful moment when you run into an ex for the first time.”

Is she referring to Joe Jonas? John Mayer? Jake Gyllenhaal? Ponder the possibilities as you watch the video.
