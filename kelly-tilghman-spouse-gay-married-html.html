The PGA Tour's first female golf announcer, and a former pro golfer, Kelly Tilghman is a broadcaster who works for The Golf Channel as a course reporter. Kelly Tilghman Age

Kelly Tilghman, also known as Kelly Ann Tilghman, was born on August 6, 1969, in North Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, the USA. She was the oldest among the five children and the only girl as well. So, growing up she was showered with a lot of love and care from her four younger brothers.
