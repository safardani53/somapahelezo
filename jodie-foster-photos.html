Photos of the Actress Hollywood Life

See photos of Oscar-winning actress Jodie Foster in honor of her birthday on November 19.

Jodie Foster is a famous award-winning actress. She got her start as a child model and made her acting debut at 6 years old. She had her breakthrough performance in the movie ‘Taxi Driver.’ Some of Jodie’s most notable movies include ‘The Accused,’ ‘Silence of the Lambs,’ ‘Contact,’ ‘Panic Room,’ ‘The Brave One,’ and ‘The Mauritanian.’ She’s done a lot of directing and producing in both film and television. Jodie has won two Oscars, three Golden Globes, and three British Academy Film Awards.

In her personal life, Jodie has two children from her relationship with producer Cydney Bernard. Jodie has been married to actress and photographer Alexandra Hedison. Jodie came out as gay in 2007. To see more photos of the acclaimed actress Jodie Foster, scroll through this gallery!
