Farmer Wants a Wife: Who is Landon Heaton?

FOX's newest reality dating show Farmer Wants a Wife premiered in March 2023.

The show introduced four farmers including, Landon Heaton, a shy cattle rancher from Oklahoma.

What is Farmer Wants a Wife?

FOX's Farmer Wants a Wife is a reality dating show that follows four single farmers who are on the search for a wife.

The farmer's potential love interests are 32 city girls who are looking to leave their metropolitan lifestyles for a shot at love.

The four farmers will bring eight women back to their ranch in hopes of finding a life-long partner.

During the teaser for the show, host, and country star, Jennifer Nettles, spoke more about the logistics of the show, saying: "They will spend six weeks getting to know each other in this journey to find love. I love love."

Who is Farmer Landon?

Landon Heaton is a 35-year-old Cattle Rancher from Alva, Oklahoma.

He owns a 300-acre Cattle Ranch and 40-acre farmhouse property.

In addition to his farm duties, Landon studied at Oklahoma State University.

When it comes to finding love, Landon is described as being extremely shy in comparison to the other farmers.
