AS an increasing number of supermarkets are hoisting up their prices, many people are turning to M&S to do their weekly food shop.

And according to one M&S fan, there are seven buys not to be missed.

Tara-Jade explained how the first item she can't go near the food store without purchasing is the 'handmade mini hoisin bao buns.'

The mini fluffy steamed buns with vegetable have a hoisin centre and as far as Tara-Jade is concerned, they're a "Wagamama dupe."

Next up is the 'Extremely cheesy 4 cheddar and red Leicester hot cross buns.'

"All I'm going to say is toasted with melted butter," she enthuses.
