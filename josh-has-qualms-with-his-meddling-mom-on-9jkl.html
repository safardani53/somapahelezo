Judy secretly sets up her son in "Heavy Meddling," airing on Monday, Jan. 15 at 9:30/8:30c on CBS and CBS All Access.

However, the lovebirds soon have an epiphany about the nature of their meeting, and it all circles back to their mothers' meddling.
