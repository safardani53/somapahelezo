Why Is Rapper Blueface Always Wearing Gloves? Details

Rapper Blueface has been seen wearing latex gloves out in public as of late. What is the reason behind his gloves? Read on for what fans think.

The Gist:

  • Rapper Blueface has been seen wearing latex gloves while out in public as of late.
  • He was spotted wearing the gloves during an interview and when he proposed to his baby mama Jaidyn Alexis in October 2023.
  • Fans are wondering why he's doing this.

Rapper Blueface (real name Jonathan Porter) is best known for his song "Thotiana" featuring Cardi B and YG. His music video for "Respect My Cryppin" also gained a lot of traction as folks were fascinated by his unique style of rapping and his distinctive face tattoos.

Besides his music, Blueface is also known for his controversial shenanigans including throwing cash on Skid Row and his past relationship with reality star Chrisean Rock.

He also has a criminal record. In October 2022, Blueface was arrested for attempted murder and discharging a gun at and/or into an occupied structure. In June 2023, he was arrested again for robbery. Both times he was released on bail.

But as of late, many fans have been curious about one of Blueface's fashion choices. He's been seen publicly wearing blue and black latex gloves and fans want to know why.

In October 2023, Blueface wore these gloves during an interview on The Jason Lee Show and also on the night he proposed to his baby mama, Jaidyn Alexis, whom he dated for nine years on and off. What gives? Let's get into it below.

So, why does Blueface keep wearing gloves?

Rapper Blueface has been seen wearing blue and black latex gloves out in public on several occasions. The reason why? It's unclear but fans have some theories. In a TikTok video, one user pointed out "Why Blueface got blue medical gloves on though? I can't be the only one to notice that."

In response to that user's video, one person in the comment section wrote that maybe Blueface is wearing gloves as a way to limit his fingerprints because of his ongoing cases.
