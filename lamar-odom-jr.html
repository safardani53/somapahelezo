Bio:
Lamar Odom Jr. (born in 2002) is is the son of former NBA player and Khloe & Lamar star, Lamar Odom. Lamar met Liza Morales when they were both attending King High School in Queens, New York. Liza and Lamar began a relationship and in 1998, the two welcomed their first child, Destiny Odom. Four years later, Lamar Jr. was born. After Lamar and Liza split, Lamar Jr. developed a positive relationship with his soon-to-be-stepmother, Khloe Kardashian. The relationship diminished after Khloe and Lamar filed for divorce.
