Strictly spoiler finally reveals who is going home tonight after BBC tries and fails to plug leak wi

THE STRICTLY spoiler account has finally leaked Saturday's results and revealed who is going home tonight - after bosses attempted to "crack down" on the show mole.

Each week an anonymous person reveals which couple has been eliminated in the hours after the Saturday live show has aired.

And they've succeeded in sharing the name of the booted star once more - albeit a lot later than usual.

They wrote on X, former Twitter, on Sunday afternoon: "The Halloween Party is over!

"Now just 2 weeks separate our remaining 9 couples from needing to pack their bucket and spade ready to freeze in the Vegas of the North (Blackpool).

"But there’s of course two more dance offs to survive.

"The BBC might want you to wait to learn the results of the latest dance off, and whilst I’ve come through a bit later than normal due to a lack of moles this week, a lack of moles never stops me and I don’t mind telling you that….."

The Sun has decided to not to spoil the result for fans.

It comes as last month BBC bosses launched an investigation to uncover the mole after the pairings were leaked.

Following their new processes the Strictly spoiler account said they were unable to leak the results.

They wrote earlier on Sunday: "Strictly Spoiler update I’ve no mole this week and other reliable sources seem to be struggling too.

"However those of you around in the pandemic will know that no moles doesn’t equal no spoiler. It does mean it might be in the morning though."

Many fans were quick to react, with one writing: "Why is it this week you have no moles weird."

Another posted: "I wish you'd posted this earlier so I didn't stay up."

A third added: "It's buggered up my day not knowing who left. It's time Angela Rippon went, it's patronising to overmark her just because she's old. Mind you, I wish I could do the splits like she does."

The leaking is possible as Sunday night's results show is filmed "live" after Saturday night's show - with the audience sworn to secrecy.

But now it's been reported show bosses are determined to uncover the person's identity after the results of each show last series were also spoiled.

A source said: "Enough is enough, it isn't fair on the fans, they look forward to tuning in to see who will be dancing together."

The TV insider continued to the Daily Mail: "Now procedures are being put in place to see if the mole can be caught though it's tricky because there are so many people involved with Strictly but the bosses are determined to nail he or she."

Last year the mole leaked which celebrity was voted off of the show each week - spoiling the results show for fans.

An insider said last year: "For years, the audience didn’t twig the Sunday show was pre-recorded, but recently results have been leaked, spoiling the suspense."
