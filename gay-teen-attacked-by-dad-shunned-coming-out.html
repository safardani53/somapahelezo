Teen’s Father Says His Son Coming Out As Gay Is ‘Worse Than Death’

A conversation between a young teen and his father from last winter has gone viral. When the boy came out as gay to his family, he received nothing but support, except from his father.

Sadly, not only did Tyler’s father not support him, but he contacted him on Facebook and said brutal things. Tyler then posted the nasty conversation on his Tumblr page.

“We took care of you since you were a baby. We loved you, took care of you when you’re sick. Lost many days and nights in all your fifteen years. Now this is what we get in return, shame and embarrassment. If only you didn’t reject God and His teachings in your life you could have been strong enough to stay from evil and scums that surrounds you. Stay away.”

After Tyler’s dad sent the first couple of messages, the teen posted the screen shot, which enraged his father further.

“Take out your post from social media.You embarrass me from all the people I knew. I’m going to puke. Whatever you do it reflects on me. People will ridicule me, insult me, and I might turn out to be a criminal. I have enough of all this bulls***. You are trying to ruin me. This is worse than death.”

So terrible.
