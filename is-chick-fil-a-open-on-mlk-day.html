Is Chick-Fil-A open on MLK Day?

CHICK-FIL-A enthusiasts are curious to know whether or not the fast food chain is open on Martin Luther King Jr Day.

The January holiday proposes a change in business hours for a number of companies, and fast food fans want to know if Chick-Fil-A is one of them.

Is Chick-Fil-A open on MLK Day?

Martin Luther King Jr Day is an American national holiday that takes place annually on every third Monday of January.

Businesses throughout the country change their hours of operation in observance of the holiday.

However, Chick-Fil-A is one company that is choosing to remain open.

The famed restaurant chain will be available to serve their customers on Monday, January 17.

What are Chick-Fil-A's hours on MLK Day?

Chick-Fil-A's popularity is due to their number of nationwide locations.

Typically, the chain is open from Monday through Saturday.

Frequent customers of the establishment are familiar with their unavailability on Sundays.

For the six days they're open, they open at 6am and close at 10pm.

For Chick-Fil-A's Martin Luther King Jr Day hours, check in with your nearest location for an accurate reflection of holiday operation times.
