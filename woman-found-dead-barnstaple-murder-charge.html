Woman, 38, found dead at home as female suspect, 32, appears in court charged with murder

A WOMAN has been charged with murder after a second woman was found dead at a home.

Hayley Adams, 38, was discovered in Barnstaple, Devon, following an "incident".

Police charged Cheyanne Loder with murder after the horror on Saturday.

She appeared at Exeter Magistrates’ Court for a brief hearing.

No further details were revealed, with Loder speaking only to confirm her name, date of birth and address.

She was remanded into custody to next appear on December 7 at Exeter Crown Court.
