Adam Collard 'gets Paige Thorne's name tattooed on him' just six weeks after meeting in Love Island

ADAM Collard appeared to get Paige Thorne’s name tattooed on his neck today.

But it was in fact just a joke made by Paige, 24, as they visited the tattoo parlour together.

Paige shared a clip of him apparently mid tattoo, writing: “As if he’s getting my name!”

Adam, 26, later cleared up the confusion, saying: "She's really chuffed with how funny she think she is.

"As much as she begged me, and begged me.

"I have only had three girls name tattooed on me, it's a curse. We decided that is not going to be the one.

"I did get a tattoo, she was a bit jealous of Tasha and Andrew getting one."

Adam previously had a Z tattooed on him for Zara McDermott, who he coupled up with during the first time in the villa.

The outing to the tattoo shop came after fans feared they had split following a weekend without each other.
