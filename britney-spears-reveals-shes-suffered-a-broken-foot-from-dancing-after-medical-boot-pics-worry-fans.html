Britney Spears reveals she's suffered a broken foot from dancing after medical boot pics worry fans

BRITNEY Spears broke her metatarsal bone in her foot when she was dancing.

The star - who concerned fans when she was seen wearing a medical boot last week - received treatment for the broken bone, according to her hunky boyfriend Sam Asghari.

“When you break something it tends to heal stronger specially when you’re my Girl👌🏽,” Asghari, 26, captioned a series of Instagram photos on Tuesday, one of which is a selfie of the pair in the hospital.

He added a cat filter and Britney has a forehead thermometer taking her temperature.

She is wearing a blue medical gown.

The caption continued, “My lioness broke her metatarsal bone on her foot doing what she loves which is dancing 💃 Wishing her the best recovery so she can jump, run, and dance her butt off 💪🏽 #stronger.”

Asghari, who is a fitness trainer, is seen in a video he shared, writing "Stronger," the name of one of her early hits, in blue and green ink on her plaster cast.

Spears, 38, can be heard telling Asghari, “I love you, baby,” in the video.

The Oops, I Did It Again songstress was seen heading to a tanning salon in Los Angeles, wearing the medical boot last week but had given no explanation.

The couple first started dating in 2016 after meeting on a music video set.

The fitness model revealed recently that he's planning to pop the question to the singer.

"I think that's the whole point of being in a relationship," he said to ET. "Otherwise why would you be in the relationship?"
