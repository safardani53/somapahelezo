Career

Carly Colon made his World Wrestling Council debut in January 2000, defeating Felix Tapia. Later, he won 17 Universal Heavyweight Championships before beating Gonzalez in the WWC Puerto Rican Heavyweight Championship. Finally, in 2003, he departed WWC to perform for Ohio Valley Wrestling for a year before signing a seven-year deal with World Wrestling Entertainment (WWE). He beat John Cena for the United States Championship in October 2004 and went on to compete with legendary wrestlers like Shawn Michaels, Triple H, Kane, and many more.

In 2005, he won the Intercontinental belt on RAW, becoming the first wrestler to win the belt in both of his debut confrontations. He later established a tag team with his brother Primo and won two straight tag team matches, including one against John Morrison and The Miz at WrestleMania XXV. He was fired from WWE in 2010 for breaking the code of conduct and went to work as a promoter on the International and Independent Circuits.
