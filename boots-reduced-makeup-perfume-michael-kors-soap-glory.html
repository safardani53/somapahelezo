BEAUTY fans, it’s time to form an orderly queue at your local Boots store.

That’s because customers are going wild after the high street store and chemist slashed the prices of posh brands, including Michael Kors, with prices starting from just £1. 

Several bargain hunters were thrilled when they found the heavily discounted products on Boots’ shelves.

These included Michael Kors perfume for just £5, MAC strobe cream for £7 and Estee Lauder mascara for a very low price of £5. 

And one customer was so happy with their bargain buys that they almost cleared the shelves and took to social media to spread the word.

Taking to Facebook group Extreme Bargains and Coupons UK, the savvy shopper said they’d timed their visit just right.

They posted a photo of their make-up and beauty haul, which was made up of seven different posh products but totalled just over £50. 

Alongside it, they wrote: “My bargains from Boots – not often I find nice offers.

“Made my day,” they added with a smiling emoji. 

She then revealed that she found the clearance items in the glass cabinets on the shop floor.

Meanwhile, another Boots customer was also all smiles when they bagged similarly good deals from their local store. 

They spotted the heavily reduced items from popular brand Soap & Glory and went to see what products took their fancy.

And in the end, they took home several – from an eye shadow palette to a plumping lip gloss.

“Found in Boots today. Eyeshadow palette was £2, everything else was £1.”

And she had a further bit of good luck when she went to the tills and realised she could use her Boots Advantage point.

She wrote: “I paid with points as well so it was all free.” 

Boots fans were very impressed with their fellow bargain hunters and took to both posts in the group to congratulate them on their finds.

Several social media users also said they would be heading to the nearest Boots to them to see if they could have similar success.
