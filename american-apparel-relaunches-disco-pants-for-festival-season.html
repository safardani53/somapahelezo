American Apparel relaunches metallic leggings in time for festival season...so would YOU be brave en

SINCE American Apparel's relaunch last month, noughties teens can now revisit fashion’s yesteryear and take a trip down metallic memory lane.

The beloved disco pant has officially made its comeback and been relaunched in six bold new shades, including Royal Blue and Deep Peacock.

  • Disco Pants, £59 from American Apparel - buy now

Long-term fans of the experimental trousers will be relieved to know that the US retailer has kept the original design that has proved popular with everyone from Selena Gomez to Michelle Keegan.

In its fashion hey-day, the colourful leggings came in every shade imaginable and were also repeatedly featured on television series Pretty Little Liars.

Sadly the price still stands at £62 for the full-leg length trouser and £49 for the bum-skimming hot-pants. Some things just never change.

For those willing to invest in the nostalgic statement piece, the wipe-clean trousers are a perfect low-maintenance choice for the upcoming festival season.

  • Metallic Halter Catsuit, £44 from American Apparel - buy now

In true American Apparel fashion, the brand has also brought back their iridescent halter neck cat-suit that is guaranteed to turn heads.

Made of 100% Polyester, be sure to pair with a transparent raincoat and body glitter for full-on festival camping chic.
