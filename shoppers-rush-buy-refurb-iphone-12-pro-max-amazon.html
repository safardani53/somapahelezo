Shoppers rush to buy refurb iPhone 12 Pro Max for less than 500 on Amazon

APPLE phones never come cheap - especially if they're the biggest handsets of the range.

But anybody for a bargain right now should see the price on the refurbished, gold-coloured iPhone 12 Pro Max, which is now a steal at £471.

UPDATE (06/02): Since we published this article, the gold iPhone 12 Pro Max has increased to £610. You can purchase the graphite model for £523.

This low price point has proved popular with Amazon shoppers: only a handful of models are still in stock.

These prices are worth comparing against those for the same model at the Apple store, where you can expect to pay £779.

All of the refurbished smartphones on sale at Amazon have been inspected, test and cleaned by trusted vendors, and they're backed by the retailer's 1-Year Renewed Guarantee

If you're worried about getting a slightly battle-scarred handset, Amazon's benchmark for refurbished items is 'showing no signs of cosmetic damage visible from a distance of 30 centimetres'.

One thing that buyers have reported on in their online reviews of the iPhone 12 Pro Max is slightly diminished battery health, with figures quoted between 83% and 94%.

Ultimately, you'll never be getting an item that's on a par with anything fresh off the production line - but taking the refurbished route is certainly a way of saving big on Apple, a brand that's pretty notorious for its prices.
