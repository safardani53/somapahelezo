Who is FBG Duck's mom, LaSheena Weekly?

LaSheena Weekly is the mother of the late rapper, FBG Duck.

Weekly has built up a following on Instagram with an accumulated 36.1k followers.

The Chicago native often uses her platform to remember her son's life and promote events that are catered to ending gun violence.

On August 1, 2023, Weekly was involved in an accident that was broadcasted live on Instagram.

What happened to FBG Duck?

On August 4, 2020, FBG Duck was fatally shot while shopping in Chicago.

The incident was also recorded revealing that Duck was attempting to hide from the shooters.

The rapper was taken to Northwestern Memorial Hospital where he was sadly pronounced dead.

In October 2021, Weekly reported via Instagram that five people have been taken into custody in relation to her son's murder.

On April 11, 2023, a sixth suspect was arrested. All members are allegedly part of a street gang called O-Block's Black Disciplines.

According to ABC 7, the six suspects were charged with "committing a murder in aid of racketeering and conspiracy to commit murder in aid of racketeering."

Who was FBG Duck?

Carlton D. Weekly, better known as FBG Duck was a rapper born on December 6, 1993, in Chicago.

He was an up-and-coming rap artist best known for songs like Slide and Exposing Me Remix. 

FBG Duck was a member of the Fly Boy Gang rap crew and was signed to Sony.

In 2019, FBG released a track called Chicago Legends, dedicated to several fellow rappers who had been killed by gun violence.
