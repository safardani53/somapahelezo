Amanda Holden shares unseen family snaps as 'charming fairy' daughter Hollie turns nine

AMANDA Holden has shared unseen family snaps as her 'charming fairy' daughter Hollie turns nine.

The 49-year-old presenter has two daughters with husband Chris Hughes, Lexi, 15 and Hollie.

To celebrate her youngest child's birthday, Amanda took to social media to share a number of sweet snaps of her over the years, some of which were being revealed for the first time.

Alongside a pretty picture of Hollie drinking a mocktail, Amanda wrote: "Our rainbow is 9 today. We love love love you #HRH our sassy, hilarious charming little fairy."

Amanda also shared pictures of Hollie with her older sister, who turned 15 earlier this week, and wrote: "My babies are growing up #sisters" followed by a heart emoji.

Other pictures featured Hollie as a baby and as a toddler as well as more recent family snaps.

Amanda was equally gushing about Lexi when she celebrated her birthday.

She wrote: "I cannot believe our baby is 15 today. Mummy and Daddy love you so much Lexi’ -you kind ,funny ,hardworking girl. We couldn’t be more proud ..."

Amanda's celeb pals including Alesha Dixon, Keith Lemon, and Tamzin Outhwaite also wished the teen a happy birthday.
