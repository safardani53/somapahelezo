Who is Wisin:

Wisin is a famous Reggaeton Singer. He was born on December 19, 1978 and his birthplace is Cayey, PR. Wisin is also well known as, Born Juan Luis Morera Luna, he is one-half of the reggaeton duo Wisin & Yandel. The band sold three million albums across the globe with their 2009 release La Revolucion.

Wisin is originated from Puerto Rico. He’s collaborated musically with artists such as Daddy Yankee, Nicky Jam, Bad Bunny, Don Omar and Ozuna, among many others.
