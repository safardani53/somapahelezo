Michelle Monaghan Baby: Actress Gives Birth to Baby Boy Tommy Francis

She's finally popped! Michelle Monaghan has given birth to her second child, her rep confirms to Us Weekly. The Source Code actress and her graphic artist husband Peter White welcomed son Tommy Francis on Wednesday, Oct. 30

Us exclusively broke news in June that the 37-year-old was expecting her second child. The couple — married since 2005 — already are parents to 4-year-old daughter, Willow Katherine.

In 2010, the brunette beauty adorably described how she balances motherhood with her career to Women's Health magazine. "On my kitchen counter sits a KitchenAid mixer, a potty seat, and a small pile of scripts," the Iowa native said during the interview. "That pretty much sums it up.
