Emilie de Ravin Gives Birth to Baby Girl!

Congratulations are in order for Emilie de Ravin.

The actress is a first-time mother!!!

The former Lost star, who now plays a key role Once Upon a Time Season 5, welcomed a little girl into the world on Saturday, March 12.

The father is de Ravin’s boyfriend, director and writer Eric Bilitch.

“Welcome to the big wide world! Vera Audrey de Ravin-Bilitch born March 12th 2016,” the actress Tweeted in announcing the life-altering news.

She added: “Couldn’t be happier! So in love with our little girl!"

de Ravin and Bilitch also used Twitter back in October to share their pregnancy with the world.

“I wanted to share some exciting news…me & @ericbilitch are expecting a baby!! ‘Tis truly a dream come true and we could not be happier,” de Ravin wrote.

Bilitch then chimed in with the following:

"Indeed! We are over the moon with joy! Could not be more excited to be starting a family with @emiliederavin."

We send our very best wishes to this newly-expanded family, while also offering this look at various celebrities who have already become parents in 2016.

Congrats to them all!!!!!
