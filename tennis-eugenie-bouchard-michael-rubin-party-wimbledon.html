'Hottest tennis player ever' Eugenie Bouchard stuns in white cut-out dress at Michael Rubin's party

TENNIS star Eugenie Bouchard wowed in a white cut-out dress as she attended businessman Michael Rubin's party.

The Canadian, 29, failed to make it through to Wimbledon's main draw after losing her qualifier 6-4 7-5 to Belgian Greet Minnen last week.

And she wasted little time leaving the UK as she jetted off to a party in New York hosted by Fanatics CEO Rubin, 50.

The 2014 Wimbledon finalist shared pictures of her dressed all in white - much like the A-List stars in attendance.

They included footballer Kylian Mbappe, reality star Kim Kardashian, NBA superstars Joel Embiid and James Harden, and British actor James Corden.

And she added the Instagram caption: "Michael did it again! Hottest party of the year!!!
