Pregnant Kate Hudson Wanted a Girl More Than Anything

Ready for a daughter! Kate Hudson revealed on Friday, April 6, that she is expecting a baby girl with her boyfriend, Danny Fujikawa, and a source tells Us Weekly exclusively that the actress couldn’t be happier.

“Kate and Danny are over-the-moon ecstatic. Kate has had the baby itch and wanted a girl more than anything,” the source explains.

“She has a special bond with her niece, Rio, and really wanted to experience having a daughter of her own,” the source adds, referring to the Bride Wars star’s brother Oliver Hudson and his wife Erinn Barlett’s 4-year-old daughter. (The couple also have two sons: Wilder, 10, and Bodhi, 8.)

Hudson, 38, started dating Fujikawa in December 2016 after meeting him 15 years earlier. She has two sons from previous relationships: Ryder, 14, whom she shares with ex-husband Chris Robinson and Bingham, 6, whose father is Hudson’s ex-fiancé, Matthew Bellamy.

“Once she met Danny she just knew that she wanted a baby with him and hoped and prayed that it would happen. For it to be a girl is just the icing on the cake,” the source tells Us. “Danny is so good with kids and has been great with her boys.”

As previously reported, Hudson shocked fans with her pregnancy news on Friday via Instagram.
