My nipple nearly died and I was in hospital for three weeks, says Real Housewives star

REAL Housewives of Cheshire star Lauren Simon has shockingly revealed that she underwent a botched boob job which nearly left her disfigured.

The 46 year old star, who is one of the original cast members of the reality TV show since 2015, opened up about her horrific ordeal during an interview with The Sun.

She candidly said: "Well, my boobs, I had them made smaller, but with one of them, my nipple nearly died. So I was really poorly. I went for a week [in hospital] and stayed three weeks."

She added: "So yeah, it was horrific. Horrific. And mentally, I had to dig deep into meditation and believing that I'd be okay. I was really poorly. It wasn't so much my confidence, funnily enough."

Viewers of the show saw Lauren, who is a firm favourite, battle through a bitter split with her property tycoon ex husband Paul Simon.

In 2018, she quit the show without an explanation but returned in 2020.

Detailing how her ordeal affected her mental health she added: " It was more my mental stress of saying I'm going to be okay, I'm going to be okay. I was so lucky. When it all went wrong, I was abroad. I got in touch with a surgeon in the UK, and he's been amazing."
