KYLIE Jenner has been accused of looking completely different at a recent event for her Bratz dolls.

Earlier this year, Kylie, 26, joined forces with Bratz to create her own line of look-alike dolls made from some of her iconic fashion looks.

However, while attending her most recent event for the brand, some critics weren't too impressed with how The Kardashians star looked.

As she showed off her dolls, the Kylie Cosmetics founder was seen wearing a curve-hugging, patterned dress with lace trim and thin straps.

The dress had a plunging neckline, allowing the reality star to show off her figure and ample cleavage.

Kylie, who has recently been sparking pregnancy rumors with her boyfriend Timothee Chalamet, had her brunette locks loosely thrown into an updo and appeared to be wearing a neutral face of makeup.

She shared a behind-the-scenes video on YouTube to showcase the event, which one viewer screenshotted and shared to an online thread to critique her unedited appearance.

"She just looks so… old," the originator wrote in the post.

Others chimed in quickly, with one saying: "She looks tired, drained, and just there for contracts only. No real excitement, no real happiness. Just tired."

A second wrote: "She looks weird af. Sorry not sorry! It’s so crazy to me that you can have a whole successful career based off your 'looks' but when people see you irl you look nothing like your pictures."
