Iza Calzado (TV Actress) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Maria Izadora Ussher "Iza" Calzado-Wintle is a Filipino actress, TV presenter, dancer and model, formerly one of the GMA Network's contract artists. After ten years, Calzado signed an exclusive contract with ABS-CBN. Calzado also signed a non-exclusive contract with Star Cinema. In 2014, she also became one of the main celebrity ambassadors for Booky, the best local food app and blog where her husband, Ben Wintle, is the CEO. In March 2017, she won the Yakushi Pearl Award for Best Performance at the Osaka Film Festival for her performance in the film Bliss.
