See Photos Of The Supermodel Hollywood Life

Madison Stubbington is an Australian model who rose to fame when she was merely a teenager. Madison was discovered at the age of 14 at a modeling competition in Adelaide. During her debut season, Madison walked the runway for a number of incredibly famous designers. 

Some of the designers she has worked with include Saint Laurent, Fendi, Dion Lee,  Giambattista Valli, Vionnet, Louis Vuitton, Jean-Paul Gaultier, Yohji Yamamoto, and Miu Miu. She has also appeared in magazines, including a spread in Vogue. Madison has an incredibly unique look, working her voluminous red hair in both print media and on the catwalk. Though still very young into her career, Madison took a step back from modeling for health reasons.
