Who are Steve Harvey's children?

FAMILY Feud host Steve Harvey has been married multiple times and has seven children with three different women.

He is best known for his various hosting duties, including The Steve Harvey Morning Show, Family Feud, and Celebrity Family Feud.

Who are Steve Harvey's children?

Steve Harvey and his first wife, Marcia, share three children: twin daughters Brandi and Karli and son Broderick Jr.

His second marriage to Mary Shackelford brought Harvey another son, Wynton.

Harvey went on to marry Marjorie Bridges in 2007.

Marjorie was already mother to two daughters, Morgan and Lori, and a son, Jason, from a previous relationship, all of whom Harvey adopted upon their nuptials.

Brandi

At 41 years old, Brandi Harvey is a multitalented entrepreneur.

According to her website, Brandi "is the Founder of Beyond Her, Podcast Host, Author, Entrepreneur, and Global Teacher who believes the journey beyond begins within."

Brandi's book, Breakthrough Sold Separately: Get Out of the Boat of Mediocrity and Walk On Water, was first released in 2019.

Karli

Karli Harvey Raymond is Brandi's twin sister.

She married her husband, Ben Raymond, in 2015.

According to her Instagram biography, Karli is a "Wife. Mother. Lover. Entrepreneur. International Event Host/Emcee. Fly girl who loves to cook tasty vegan food!"

Broderick Jr

Born on April 29, 1991, Broderick Harvey Jr is a fashion designer and entertainer.

The 32-year-old launched his label - Need Money Not Friends - in 2010 and has since followed in his father's footsteps by becoming a presenter on television.

Wynton

Born on July 18, 1997, Wynton is the youngest Harvey child.

With over 92K followers on Instagram, the 25-year-old has cemented himself as a social media icon.

As a professional photographer, Wynton works to document "everything from landscapes, portraits, high-fashion, documentation and products to conceptual shoots of household objects," per his official website.

Morgan

Morgan Harvey Hawthorne is the third oldest, following twins Brandi and Karli.

With over 93K followers on Instagram, Morgan often shares photos of her husband and children on their world travels.

She and her husband, Kareem Hawthorne, are members of the team behind Lift Every Voice and Meme, an adult-rated card game curated to celebrate Black culture.

Jason

On March 23, 2022, Jason Harvey debuted his first fashion line, entitled Eden on Hush.

The 32-year-old is the designer behind the creative house Worldly, based in Atlanta, Georgia.

Describing his collection, Jason noted it as "a unique way to let that light shine, that sense of Eden," via The Atlanta Journal-Constitution.

Lori

Born on January 13, 1997, Lori Harvey is a model and socialite.

She founded the skincare company SKN by LH in 2021.
