Angelina Jolie's Kids Are All Grown Up at 'Kung Fu Panda 3' Premiere: Photo

Where did their babies go?! Angelina Jolie brought along some very special guests to the L.A. premiere of her new movie, Kung Fu Panda 3, on Saturday, January 16. The Oscar winner walked the red carpet with five of her six children — Pax, 12, Zahara, 11, Shiloh, 9, and twins Vivienne and Knox, 7.

The beaming mom, 40 — married to Brad Pitt — kept a close eye on her kids on the red carpet. Brangelina’s eldest son, Maddox, who was a production assistant on their drama By the Sea, was not in attendance. (Pitt, 52, recently attended and presented at the 73rd Annual Golden Globe Awards in L.A. on Sunday, January 10.)

The group posed for photos at the star-studded event, where they rubbed elbows with Jolie’s costars Jack Black and Kate Hudson. Pax, who broke his leg during the family’s Thailand vacation last month, walked on crutches. Zahara wore an all-black outfit and donned a large hair barrette. Shiloh rocked a gray vest and pants, while little Vivienne looked adorable in a white top and beige bottoms. She kept close to her famous mom and brother Knox.

Jolie has been busy promoting her new movie, and the cast is currently in Shanghai. Earlier this month, the actress revealed that their kids voiced panda characters in the animated flick.

“They were kind of shy,” she told Entertainment Tonight at the time. “They don’t really want to be actors, but I didn’t want them to miss the opportunity. They came in, and they had a lot of fun with it.”
