Cole Bennett Biography

Cole Bennett is an American Youtuber, business executive, videographer, and music video director. Cole is the founder of the multimedia company and earned fame from his company. Bennett also has a youtube channel and has 20.6 million subscribers on his Youtube channel . 

Born And Raised In Plano, Illinois

Cole Bennet was born on 14th May 1996, in Plano, Illinois, United States. He belongs to the white ethnicity and holds American nationality. 

Cole was raised along with his brother Lil Jake and sister by his parents in their home town Illinois. 

Cole Bennett moved to Chicago along with his family. Cole was studying at DePaul University and was passionate about film making and he dropped out of the University. His mother supported him and bought him a camera.
