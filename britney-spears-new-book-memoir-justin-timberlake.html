Five Britney Spears memoir bombshells from sex life revelations before first dates to post-abortion

BRITNEY Spears’ bombshell new memoir hasn't been published yet, but it's already sent shockwaves around the world.

The Gimme More star, 41, is set to lay bare her love life, family rifts, and heartbreaking struggles under a decade-long controversial mental health conservatorship.

The Woman In Me won’t be available to the public until October 24, but shocking details began to leak earlier this week.

Here The U.S. Sun breaks down some of the most sensational new revelations that fans can expect to find in Britney’s tell-all tome.

JUSTIN TIMBERLAKE'S BREAK-UP LETTER

Britney reveals that after the "love of her life" Justin Timberlake split up with her via text in 2002, he traveled to her home in Louisiana to hand her a letter he had written.

The pop superstar says that following the breakup she stopped talking to anyone and barely left her house.

She was so distraught that she spent her days lying in bed and staring at the ceiling.

Britney tells how Justin visited her in her hometown of Kentwood and brought with him a lengthy letter that he had written and gotten framed.

Explaining that it still makes her want to cry when she thinks about it, she says that Justin wrote: "I can’t breathe without you."

Britney’s first thought was how good of a writer Justin was. He had captured exactly what she was feeling, too.

The pop star says she remained in a "trance" and unable to speak with anyone after reading the letter.

In a heartbreaking revelation, Britney said she still keeps the letter under her bed to this day.

'SH*TFACED' FIRST WEDDING IN VEGAS

Britney’s notorious Las Vegas shotgun wedding to childhood pal Jason Alexander in January 2004 left her fans stunned.

Her first marriage was a bolt out of the blue and lasted less than three days.

Jason, 42, has previously admitted that he and Britney had been drinking before deciding to go to Sin City’s famous Little White Chapel to tie the knot.

Although she doesn’t refer to Jason by name, Britney speaks publicly about the episode for the first time ever in the book.

She says she was hanging out with friends at the guesthouse she’d had built at her family’s home in Louisiana during the Christmas holidays when her mother Lynne got annoyed with them for making too much noise.

Deciding she had enough money to do whatever she wanted, Britney took her pals to stay at the Palms Casino Resort in Vegas.

Britney admits that she and her friends "got phenomenally stupid" during the getaway.

She also relates how her pal Paris Hilton showed up and after a few drinks, they ended up on top of tables with their shoes off.

Britney says she wound up in bed with an "old friend" – a reference to Jason.

She goes on to tell how on the third night they got "s**tfaced" together before having the "brilliant idea" of going to the Little White Chapel.

The pop superstar claims that when they got there another couple was getting married so she and Jason had to wait in line.

Britney writes: "People have asked me if I loved him. To be clear: he and I were not in love. I was just honestly very drunk - and probably, in a more general sense at that time in my life, very bored."

The marriage lasted a total of 55 hours before being annulled under the pretext that Britney "lacked understanding of her actions" when she agreed to get married.

Jason made headlines years later after he was arrested for crashing another wedding – Britney’s third marriage at her home in Thousand Oaks, California, to now ex-husband Sam Asghari in June last year.

TOTAL CONTROL OF HER SEX LIFE

Following a very public meltdown, Britney was placed in a controversial mental health conservatorship under her father Jamie in February 2008.

In the book, she details how her father and management team controlled her until she was released from the conservatorship in November 2021.

In one of the most shocking revelations, Britney claims that this included strict control of who she was allowed to date.

She says that before she went on a first date with any man, the potential suitor would be told her medical and sexual history.

"When someone wanted to date me, the security team who answered to my father would run a background check on him, make him sign an NDA, and even make him submit a blood test," Britney added.

BRITNEY AND JUSTIN'S ABORTION

The biggest bombshell Britney drops in the book is that she aborted her and Justin Timberlake’s baby around 2000 when they were both about 19.

Following "heart-wrenching discussions," the former couple – who dated from 1999 to 2002 – agreed not to go ahead with the pregnancy.

Britney writes in her memoir: "Justin definitely wasn’t happy about the pregnancy. He said we weren’t ready to have a baby in our lives, that we were too young."

She also reveals that they decided not to go to the hospital for the abortion so that no one would find out.

Britney says that she took "little pills" and that the pain was "excruciating."

The decision clearly still haunts her even though she went on to have her sons Sean Preston in 2005 and Jayden James in 2006 with her ex-husband Kevin Federline.

Justin, 42, went on to marry The Illusionist actress Jessica Biel, 41, and the couple share sons Silas, 8, and Phineas, 3.

A source told The U.S. Sun on Tuesday that the revelations will have left Justin and his family reeling.

The insider, a longtime family friend, said: "This book is a nightmare for him because of how it throws the whole family into chaos."

CHEATING ON JUSTIN WITH WADE ROBSON

On Wednesday, The U.S. Sun revealed that Britney finally admits in the book that she cheated on Justin Timberlake with dancer Wade Robson.

Australian-born Robson, 41, made headlines years later when he accused pop superstar Michael Jackson of sexually abusing him.

Gossip had swirled for years that Britney split up with Justin in 2002 after getting with Wade.

It had been suspected that Timberlake wrote his hit song Cry Me a River about Spears cheating on him.

In her memoir, Britney claims that she did cheat with the choreographer after finding out that Justin had slept around behind her back.

She writes of her fling with Wade: "We were out one night and we went to a Spanish bar. We danced and danced. I made out with him that night."
