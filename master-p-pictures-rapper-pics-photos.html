Master P Pictures See Photos Of The Rapper Hollywood Life

Master P (born Percy Miller Sr.) is an American rapper, record executive, actor, and entrepreneur. He’s best known as the founder of No Limit Records. He found success in the late 1990s with his single, “Make ‘Em Say Uhh!” He has acted in films like ‘Gone In 60 Seconds,’ and appeared as a professional wrestler in World Championship Wrestling in 1999. While he hasn’t had much mainstream success in the decades following his breakout, he continued to release music. Master P has also maintained his life as a record executive and entrepreneur, building a diverse portfolio of investments. In 2013, his net worth was estimated to be about $200 million.

Master P is also a prolific lover, having fathered nine children. His son, Romeo Miller, performed as Lil Romeo in late ‘90s/early 2000s hip-hop. In 2022, he lost his daughter, Tytyana, to a fentanyl overdose. Her struggles with addiction were captured on ‘Growing Up Hip Hop.’
