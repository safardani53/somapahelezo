Is Salt Bae married and does he have children?

RESTAURANTEUR and internet sensation Salt Bae first hit the spotlight back in 2017 - after he became the face of a viral meme.

But is he married and does he have children? Here's everything you need to know.

Who is Salt bae?

Nusret Gökçe is the man behind the nickname - Salt Bae.

Born in 1983, Salt Bae is a Turkish butcher, chef, food entertainer and restaurateur - whose technique for preparing and season meat became a widely circulated meme back in 2017.

He was originally born in Erzurum to a Kurdish family - his father Faik, who was a mineworker and his mother Fatma.

However, during his time in the sixth grade, he was forced to leave school and work as a butcher's apprentice in Istanbul - due to the family's struggle with finances.

Between 2007 and 2010, Nusret visited a number of different countries, including Argentina and the United States, where he worked in local restaurants for free - hoping to gain experience as a cook and restaurateur.

In 2010, Salt Nusret opened his first restaurant in Istanbul and just four years later opened his second in Dubai.

But it wasn't until 2017 that the name Salt Bae shot to fame overnight - through a series of viral internet videos and memes that show him uniquely cutting meat and sprinkling salt.

Due to the extent of exposure he gained after becoming a viral internet sensation - he now owns Nusr-Et, a chain of luxury steak houses.

As of 2021, he has Nusr-Et branches in Turkey, Greece, the United States, the United Kingdom, the United Arab Emirates, Qatar, and Saudi Arabia.

His net worth is estimated between $50 to $70 million.

Is Salt Bae married?

If you've visited Salt Bae's Instagram you will notice that he is constantly surrounded by beautiful female diners in many of his posts - and happily flirting with them.

But mystery around the Turkish chefs love life remains - as he tends to keep this part of his personal life very private and away from the spotlight.

He once claimed he was too busy for a relationship - getting up at 3am to go to meat markets and getting just five hours sleep at night.

He said: “I'm so concentrated on meat that I can't see a woman or anything.

“I don't have any time for a girlfriend.”

Does Salt Bae have any children?

Just like his love life, Salt Bae's family life is also surrounded by mystery - and fans of the chef are eager to know if he really has nine children.

The speculation came after he posted a candid snap of himself and nine young children back in September 2016.

The snap pictured Salt Bae holding a newborn baby whilst nine other children stood closely around him.

He captioned the picture 'The man who didn't spend time with his family wasn't a real man' - and this sent fans wild with speculation in the comment section.

One curious Instagram user wrote: ''Does he really have nine kids???''
