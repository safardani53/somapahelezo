Cheryl's ex Liam Payne helped her buy 3.7m Buckinghamshire mansion where she lives with their son

LIAM Payne forked out the big bucks to help his ex Cheryl buy her stunning mansion in Buckinghamshire.

Cheryl, 37, lives in the decadent home worth £3.7million with their son Bear, three.

Title deeds for the home show that her One Direction ex helped her pay for the property before she moved in March, 2019.

The title deeds name Liam James Payne as one of two lenders along with Accord Mortgages Ltd, according to reports in the Daily Mail.

The registered owner of the home is listed under the Fight For This Love singer's maiden name, Cheryl Ann Tweedy.

Liam and Cheryl split in 2018 after two years of dating, but not before she gave birth to the first child for either of them in 2017.

Cheryl lived with Bear at Liam's £5.1million six-bedroom Surrey property which they had shared as a couple for nine months after they split.

The singer then relocated to her current three storey home in March 2019, while Liam lives in London.

He has not moved back to the Surrey house, but is renting it out for £8,000 a week.

The Girls Aloud star and mum of one is notoriously private about her home life with Bear, but recently gave her fans a glimpse at their gorgeous house.

She took to her Instagram Stories on Tuesday to film a video of her perfectly-adorned tree.

Set against the Winter Wonderland tune, Cheryl firstly honed in on the green fir decked out with gold, bronze and deep purple baubles before panning the camera onto a Father Christmas figure sat alongside.

She added a sparkling filter and tagged the Instagram handle of floral designers, Early Hours London Ltd.

A festive pine cone and fir tree border, studded with gleaming baubles, was then seen hanging off furniture.

Earlier this year, she partially broke the privacy surrounding the tot as she featured a video on her Instagram page featuring his voice - and his Geordie accent.
