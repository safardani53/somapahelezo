See Khloe Kardashians most lavish indulgences including $450K Rolls Royce- but there's an even mo

KHLOE Kardashian has garnered quite a collection of luxury items throughout her time in the public eye.

The Hulu star, who has been accused of flaunting her wealth, has spared no expense when outfitting herself and her family with the finest things that life has to offer.

The reality TV star's success has afforded her access to and the ability to purchase a $450,000 Rolls Royce and an exclusive Hermes bag, among other pricey items.

LUXURY VEHICLES

Last year, Khloe, 39, added a bright blue Lamborghini Urus to her already sizable fleet of luxury vehicles.

The Good American co-founder showed off her new wheels on an episode of her Hulu reality series, The Kardashians.

Lamborghini’s Urus pushes the boundaries of traditional SUV form and function with its super-car-like performance that’s housed in a practical frame.

Lamborghini describes its Urus’ performance, saying it's “powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing 650 CV and 850 Nm of torque, Urus accelerates from 0 to 62 mph in 3.6 seconds and reaches a top speed of 190 mph.”

The Urus gives you the speed you’d typically seek in a Lamborghini sports car while allowing you the opportunity to explore a wide range of terrains.

The Urus, which Khloe's sisters Kylie, 26, and Kendall, 27 also own, costs around $218,000.

Soon after Khloe debuted her brand new Lamborghini SUV, the reality TV star picked up a Rolls-Royce Ghost Black Badge.

Khloe already owned both a Rolls-Royce Cullinan and a Rolls-Royce Phantom, according to The Richest.

The twin-turbocharged V12 Cullinan is priced at about $350,000 while the equally powered Rolls-Royce Wraith set the TV personality back about $450,000.

Khloe purchased the new ride after she and her on-again-off-again baby daddy, Tristan Thompson, 32, welcomed their second child together, Tatum, now one.

The Rolls-Royce Ghost Black Badge costs approximately $500,000 and has a 0 to 62 mph time of just 4.8 seconds.

BIRKIN BAG

Earlier this year, Khloe was photographed in distressed jeans with over $125,000 in purses draped over her arm.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum was spotted leaving a studio in Calabasas, California, in photos exclusively obtained by The U.S. Sun.

The apparel brand founder was wearing extremely ripped jeans with tears from the top of her thighs to just below her knees.

The light-wash denim jeans also had slits in the front of the pant leg to show off the Los Angeles native's white sneakers.

The jeans appeared to be several sizes too big for the reality star as they were practically falling off her hips and her legs were nearly invisible under the fabric.

On top, she maintained her extremely casual vibe with a simple white zip-up hoodie.

She wore her blond hair half up, and she accessorized the outfit with a large pair of black sunglasses.

To finish off the look, Khloe carried around two bags to remind everyone that her style is anything but casual.

The first bag was the extremely popular Medium Dior Book Tote from the French fashion house, Dior.

The boxy tote bag with a unique jungle design sells for $3,350 on Dior's website.

The second bag Khloe was carrying was the real show stopper.

It was a matte alligator Birkin bag with gold hardware in a pretty seafoam green color.

The same bag is currently listed on a resale website for $125,000.

PLAY-MANSION

This month, Khloe showed off her over-the-top playhouse for her daughter True, five, as she gave a glimpse of the extravagant exterior of the play area on Snapchat.

The playhouse was set in the luxurious gardens of Khloe's $17million Los Angeles mansion.

The pretend home looked big enough to replace an actual small family home.

Unlike most playhouses made out of plastic or rustic wood, the exterior featured smooth wooden panels, metal details, and glass windows.

The area was surrounded by trees, which had been expertly groomed back from the entrance.

Approaching the playhouse, three sturdy steps led up to a path toward the front door.

The door had a metal knocker and handle, while a life-size mailbox sat at its side.

The kids' play den had two floors, with what looked like a balcony coming out from the top level.

Khloe's post showing off the luxury toy comes after the star was criticized for her "wasteful" parenting.

FLASHING THE CASH

For her daughter's fifth birthday, she gave True an Octonauts-themed bash.

The party included a two-tier birthday cake, decorated with blue icing and covered with detailed characters from the kids' series.

In a video, taken by Khloe's sister Kylie, an entire underwater display was captured, filled with clear and colored balloons mimicking jellyfish floating around.

There was also a massive balloon display, made to look like a pink octopus wrapped around a cabana beside the in-ground pool.

She then showed off a wall of personalized backpacks, which Khloe offered to the kids as favors.

Critics complained about the show of wealth on Reddit, Instagram, and TikTok less than 24 hours after the party had ended.

One person wrote: "Ooooh to be this rich and put on these types of birthdays for my kids."

Another commented: "Birthday parties must be so fun when you're rich."

A third sarcastically added: "I love watching rich people enjoy their lives!"

While someone else quipped: "Here’s a 'little peak' at my garish, wasteful party that my child won’t remember, filled with food that will never be eaten and crafts that will never get fully completed."

Although fans were concerned about the little ones and their broken arms, many were furious at Khloe for filtering the children for her own aesthetic purposes.

GUCCI GOWN

Around the same time as the birthday party, True posed in a $1,200 pink Gucci dress for Easter.

The mom-of-two shared a variety of pictures of True on her Instagram.

True was all smiles in the photos while throwing her hands in the air and posing with peace signs.

She sported an all-pink ensemble including a bow headband on her head as well as the Gucci dress.

As for jewelry, True showed off a necklace with her name on it as well as a gold cross. She also wore two bracelets on her arms.

CALABASAS ABODE

Beyond True's playhouse, Khloe has boasted the beauty that lies behind her $17million dollar mansion in Calabasas, California.

Khloe recently released a few snaps on Snapchat of her picturesque backyard that looked like it could be featured in Home & Gardens.

Photos included in the social media thread flaunted her luxuriously large pool.

Surrounding her outdoor swimming area was several plush cream-colored lounge chairs.

Located near the pool was a splendid cabana filled with a flat-screen television and chairs that coordinated with the ones around the pool.

Near her pool was also a square concrete fire pit that had a set of chairs around it.

Towering trees flooded her backyard, and healthy green grass covered its perimeter.

The mom of two even had a toy ram and toy lamb situated on her patio for her kids, True and Tatum to play with.

Khloe actually shared two pics of True, one of her petting the fake ram, and another of her stroking the faux lamb.

The Hulu personality never seems to miss an opportunity to post about her home, especially the back of it.

The Kardashians star took to her Snapchat again last month to share pictures of her sprawling backyard.

In them, Khloe captured a ton of fish swimming in a pond and an arch over a walkway into the yard.

She also gave an up-close view of her rose garden beside her massive in-ground swimming pool.

After a lengthy building process, Khloe moved into the home in November 2021.
