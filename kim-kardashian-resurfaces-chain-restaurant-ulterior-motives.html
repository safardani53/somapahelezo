KARDASHIAN fans have been left in shock after Kim returned to social media with an Instagram post from a chain restaurant.

Critics are skeptical of the star's post, however, suggesting she has "ulterior motives."

Kim, 43, stunned her followers when she shared an Instagram Story post from inside a P.F. Changs.

The star snapped a photo of her long fingernails holding a piece of paper – seemingly from a fortune cookie.

It read: "The day is near when you share your talent with the world."

The Skims mogul captioned the post: "Finally."

Her snap was shared on a Kardashian-themed Reddit, where the original poster wrote: "Kim at a chain restaurant? I'm shocked she would dare be seen there."

A second commenter speculated: "Another ad."

"PF Changs is def 'beneath' her or so she thinks," a third person wrote.

One person defended: "Remember when Kim, North, Tracy [Romulus] and co. were celebrating North's basketball game at Red Lobster and she learned that she passed the baby bar? She loves a chain restaurant."

SIMPLE TASTE

While there have been questions about Kim's motives for visiting P.F. Changs, the Hulu star is known to pop in at chain eateries.

In November 2022, after snapping photos on her lavish private jet in a tiny rhinestone ensemble, the reality star and her crew headed to In-N-Out in Calabasas, California.

Kim donned a bejeweled bra and matching choker, showing off her toned physique.

She had her long blonde hair hanging down over her shoulders.

The Kardashians star brought her sister Khloe Kardashian, mom Kris Jenner, and members of her inner circle along as they ordered burgers, fries, and other tasty menu items from the chain eatery.

The trip was documented for the Hulu reality show as well as on social media.

Kim and her friends took shots as they finished their meals.

She popped back in at the restaurant in March of that year, this time with her then-boyfriend Pete Davidson.

Kim and Pete also visited a Cheesecake Factory with her kids.

The New York comic was seen holding hands with her son Saint, eight.

TRYING TOO HARD?

Kim lives a lavish life, frequently showing off her impressive luxuries on social media.

Occasionally, however, she documents more relatable moments – sometimes facing criticism for it.

In early 2023, the SKKN founder was slammed for her attempt to appear relatable during a trip to London.

Kim posed with a pint of beer in a pub and appeared in another snap in a red photo booth.

Critics called the pics "cringy," tearing her apart in the comments.

One wrote, "Running out of content? It's getting cringy," while a second added: "Why are her posts so corny."
