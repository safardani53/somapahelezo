Remaining U.S. Eras Tours dates

Taylor Swift has performed for multiple cities during The Eras Tour thus far and fans who are trying to secure tickets to the show are running out of opportunities.

The Eras Tour remaining shows include:

  • Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania (tonight and 17)
  • Minneapolis, Minnesota (June 23 and 24)
  • Cincinnati, Ohio (June 30 and July 1)
  • Kansas City, Missouri (July 7 and 8)
  • Denver, Colorado (July 14 and 15)
  • Seattle, Washington (July 22 and 23)
  • Santa Clara, California (July 28 and 29)
  • Inglewood, California (August 4, 5, 8, and 9)

Resale tickets available to fans

Popular resell account, @ErasTourResell, is asking fans to get extra tickets to resell for this weekend's concerts.

The account has already managed to resell two tickets for tomorrow's concert.

This account encourages fans to be aware of accounts that seek to scam others.

Only verified fans can access available tickets

Much like the queue last night, only verified fans of Taylor Swift can purchase tickets right now on Ticketmaster.

Those who are not verified will find the following message on their screens: “The account you’re using is not linked to this invitation-only sale. To continue, please switch accounts and sign in with the same account that you received your sale notification.

“Didn’t receive an invite to this sale? Please check back for more updates.”

Acrisure Stadium shares what to know for concerts

Taylor Swift's shows this weekend take place at Pittsburgh's Acrisure Stadium, and they have shared "what to know before you go" to the concert.

According to their post, parking lots open at 1.30pm, gates open at 4.30pm, and the show itself starts at 6.30pm.

The venue has a Mobile Ticket Only policy, so fans are encouraged to download their tickets before arriving.

It also has a Clear Bag policy in effect, where fans can only bring see-through bags into the stadium.

Pittsburgh Acrisure Stadium seat map

Ticketmaster has made the seat map to Acrisure Stadium available to those who have managed to access the queue to that venue.
