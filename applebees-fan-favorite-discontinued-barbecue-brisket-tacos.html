Applebee's confirms fan-favorite appetizer is discontinued but suggests 'variety' of alternatives

FANS have complained to Applebee's on Twitter after a fan-favorite was no longer on the menu.

Applebee's, a casual dining restaurant chain, confirmed to fans that the appetizer is officially discontinued.

One customer tweeted that they wanted the Barbecue Brisket Tacos back on the menu.

"What’s a guy gotta do to get the bbq brisket tacos back on the menu," the customer tweeted.

Applebee's responded, saying they have plenty of other items the customer can enjoy in their restaurants.

"We’re sorry that you’re missing our BBQ Brisket Tacos," the company responded.

"We like to keep a variety of appetizers on our menu and change up the selection every so often."

Applebee's also reassured the customer that their complaint was passed on to other decision-makers.

TACO HEAVEN

Applebee's describes the now-defunct tacos as a blend of "southwest" inspired flavors.

In a item description, the company described the tacos: "Bursting with big southwest flavor, flour tortillas are grilled then filled with shredded beef barbacoa, sweet Memphis BBQ sauce, a blend of cheddar cheeses and pico de gallo."

"Topped with crispy onions, a drizzle of our Mexi-ranch sauce and cilantro."

Customers also received a side of coleslaw with their orders.

The all-day tacos were served for $8.

The tacos are just one of Applebee's offerings taken off the restaurant menu.

OTHER APPLEBEE'S DISCONTINUED ITEMS

Another fan favorite, the Grilled Chicken Wonton Tacos vacated the restaurant's menus in 2020.

Customers have complained about the appetizer's disappearance on multiple social media sites.

One TikToker captioned a video: "Applebee's needs to put this back on their menu."

Another fan yelled on Twitter: THIS PANDEMIC HAS STRUCK A FATAL BLOW TO ONLY THING I HELD DEAR. CHICKEN WANTON TACOS FROM APPLEBEES."

The sweet and spicy sriracha shrimp appetizer also appears to have disappeared from the menu.

Customers are upset about its absence as well.

"I loved Applbees [sic] Sriracha Shrimp so much," someone Tweeted.

"My taste buds exploded and the world got still and quiet! My fiancée was talking but I couldn't hear him! Bring it back! Please!!!"
