WRESTLER Harley Cameron posed in a sizzling photo shoot for the front cover of Fitness Gurls that sent Instagram into meltdown.

Cameron, who is also a singer as well as a model, was pictured in the magazine with a raft of different barely-there bikinis alongside other models.

The Australian has been keeping herself busy as she recently paired up with Bare Knuckle FC which, as the title suggests, is a promotion that hosts bare-knuckle boxing fights.

She claims she enjoys every minute of it while working as a ring card girl.

At the same time, she continues her passion with professional wrestling because it makes her feel "so alive".

Cameron told Fitness Gurls: "It’s amazing [Bare Knuckle FC]! It’s definitely a very intense sport and having a front-row seat at every event as a ring card girl has been incredible.
