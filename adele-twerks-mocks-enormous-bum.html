Adele Twerks, Mocks &quot;Enormous Bum&quot;

Adele is currently on a world tour, drawing sold out crowds at arenas throughout Europe who have bought tickets to hear the best singer on the planet do her talented thing.

Over the past few weeks, however, fans haven’t simply been treated to such hits as "When We Were Young" and "Hello."

They’ve seen Adele help one supporter propose to another.

They’ve seen her sing a duet with an Autistic fan.

And then, most recently, they’ve seen her make like Miley Cyrus or Rihanna and Twerk. Sort of!

“I think I just twerked,” Adele says in the video below after doing a little dance, laughing as a crowd in London screams with excitement.

“I can’t do it that well. My whole body has to move – watch!”

She then does the dance move again and can’t help but crack up at herself in the process.

“My bum could break my back it’s so enormous," she quips to close the clip.

Seriously, Adele. Stop being so awesome. You’re making it impossible for the rest of us!
