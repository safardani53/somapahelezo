Robert Norman Reiner is an American actor, comedian and filmmaker. As an actor, Reiner became nationally popular for the first time as Michael Stivic in the CBS sitcom All in the Family, which earned him two Primetime Emmy Awards. As a director, Reiner has been recognized by the US Director Award with nominations for the teen drama Stand by Me, the romantic comedy When Harry Met Sally ... and the military courtroom drama A Few Good People, the latter also earned it an Oscar nomination for Best Picture. He also received four nominations for the Golden Globe Award for Best Director.
