Who is Marcus Wareing:

Marcus Wareing is a famous Chef. He was born on June 29, 1970 and his birthplace is Southport, England. He has worked as the head chef at his Michelin starred restaurant called Marcus.

Marcus is originated from England. He met chef Gordon Ramsay at the London restaurant Le Gavroche where they both worked in 1993.
