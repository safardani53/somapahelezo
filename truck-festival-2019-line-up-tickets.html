When is Truck Festival 2019, what's the line-up and how can I get tickets?

TRUCK Festival is back for another year and fans can't wait for it.

Here's everything you need to know about the event including its line-up and tickets.

When is Truck Festival 2019?

Truck Festival will take off on July 26 and conclude on July 28 this year.

Located in Oxfordshire's Hill Farm, the event will welcome several big names in music.

It is one of the longest running and most popular small festivals of the UK.

It was started in 1998 by the Bennett family who felt that festivals like Glastonbury has become too commercial and predictable.

What's the line-up?

Headliners of the festival have been announced as Wolf Alice, Foals, and Two Door Cinema Club.

Slaves, Nothing but Thieves, You Me At Six and Don Broco will also take the event by storm as a headliner.

Here are the other artists you can expect to see at the festival.

  • Idles
  • Kate Nash
  • Shame
  • Lewis Capaldi
  • The Futureheads
  • Palace
  • Sea Girls
  • Ten Tonner
  • Fontaines D.C.
  • Spector
  • Clean Cut Kid
  • She Drew The Gun
  • Easy Life
  • Cassia
  • Whenyoung
  • Sports Team
  • Milk Teeth
  • Puppy
  • Only the Poets

But that's not all, from Heavy Lungs to Annabel Allum many more artists will take the stage.

How can I get tickets?

Tickets to the festival can be bought via the event's official website.

Tier 2 tickets, costing £105,  have sold out.

However, Tier 3 tickets, costing £110, are still on sale.

For those who can't pay in full at once, They also have a pre-payment plan that offers monthly payments.
