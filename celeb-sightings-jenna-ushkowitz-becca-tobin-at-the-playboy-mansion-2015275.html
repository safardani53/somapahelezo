Celeb Sightings: Jenna Ushkowitz, Becca Tobin at the Playboy Mansion!

—Jenna Ushkowitz and Becca Tobin had a girls' night out watching an advance screening of Entourage at the Playboy Mansion in L.A.

—Gwen Stefani wore BaubleBar at An Evening with Women benefitting The Los Angeles LGBT Center in Hollywood.

—Kelly Rutherford picked up a Bedgear Performance Pillow at URBO in Midtown, NYC.

—Justin Bieber gave an impromptu performance of Boyz II Men’s “I’ll Make Love To You” at Jin&Quinn’s Sunday Jazz night at the W Hollywood.

—Keke Palmer joined Get Schooled to recognize top students in the Capital One Bank NYC College Challenge in NYC.

—Beverley Mitchell teamed up with RoseArt to create a football field-sized chalk-paint mural at the Santa Monica Pier in addition to donating $50,000 to the Boys & Girls Club of America to support club art programs coast-to-coast this summer.

—Cheryl Burke talked about how she encountered depression after leaving Dancing with the Stars on the set of her new NBC competition show I Can Do That, airing Tuesdays at 10/9(c) through June 30.

—Nicole Scherzinger grabbed a late bite with a group of friends at Dos Caminos Meatpacking, where they dined on ceviche and cocktails in NYC.

–NBA star Dwyane Wade ordered two burgers without buns, in accordance with his diet, at Junior’s Restaurant in Times Square, NYC.

—Camila Alves stopped by the MAGNUM Ice Cream lounge in Cannes and wore a stunning BCBGMAXAZRIA for MAGNUM Belgian Chocolate Wrap, an accessory infused with the irresistible aroma of Belgian chocolate.

—Emma Roberts shopped at Zadig & Voltaire’s Soho store in NYC.

—Vanessa Hudgens wore a Kenneth Cole Leather Fringe Sling Bag while walking her dog Darla in NYC.

—Ashley Greene waited for her new Casper Mattress to be delivered to her home in L.A.

—Porsha Williams celebrated Memorial Day weekend at the Barceló Bávaro Palace Deluxe Hotel in the Dominican Republic.

—Marky Ramone hung out with producer and actor Danny A. Abeckaser during the premiere of Club Life at Avenue where guests snacked on Artichoke pizza and drank Stella Artois in NYC.

—Chance The Rapper sipped cocktails during Vashtie's DJ set at Cerise, the restaurant and lounge atop of Virgin Hotel Chicago.

—50 Cent, taking a break from promoting his upcoming films Southpaw and Spy, enjoyed a night out with friends at CRUSH at the MGM Grand in Las Vegas.

—Harry Styles and the Chickstarter Andrea Horblitt had a business dinner at Ysabel in West Hollywood.
