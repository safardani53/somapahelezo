The bold outfit in London came just one week after the former E! star attended a New York Fashion Week and showed off her style in the front row.

The 25-year-old headed to NYC's Fifth Avenue in Midtown to see the Retrofête SS24 show.

For the event, the influencer stunned in a dark, sparkling outfit.

Jordyn wore a long blond wig, with her hair reaching her ribs in loose waves.

She donned chic sunglasses but removed them to show off her flawless makeup, with swooping eyeliner and plenty of peachy blush.

For her outfit, the star opted for a maroon set covered in sequins.

Jordyn went for a tiny bra top which seemed slightly too small, showing off her cleavage and underboob as the fabric stretched over her chest.

The plunging bra showed off plenty of her chest and left her toned stomach completely bare.

She added a matching blazer but wore the piece over her shoulders to emphasize the shirt underneath.

Finishing the look, the celebrity sported sparkling wide-leg pants, along with pointed-toe heels.

For jewelry, Jordyn kept things simple with just a delicate crystal necklace.
