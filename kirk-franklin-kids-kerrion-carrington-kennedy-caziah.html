Who are Kirk Franklin's kids?

GOSPEL star Kirk Franklin has posted an apology video after being slammed by fans over a shocking clip his son Kerrion shared.

The sixteen-time Grammy Award winner said his son "had a toxic relationship" with the rest of the family.

Who are Kirk Franklin kids?

Kirk Franklin, 51, has four children - Kerrion, Carrington, Kennedy and Caziah.

When Kirk married his wife, Tammy Collins in 1996, they both had a child from a previous relationship.

The gospel artist had son Kerrion and Tammy had daughter Carrington, whom Kirk legally adopted later on.

The couple had two children together, Kennedy and Caziah.

Kerrion Franklin

Kerrion, 33, is the gospel artist's eldest son.

Kerrion, who was born in 1988, describes himself as an "author, producer, and cinematographer" on his Instagram page.

He is also into music and often shares pictures in a studio.

He hit the headlines in March 2021 after sharing a video of an argument between him and his father.

Kerrion captioned the 45-second clip "This is why I’m done. No father should speak to their children like this.

"If I have any issues it’s because of this type of treatment that I deal with behind closed doors."

On Saturday, March 13, 2021, his father Kirk posted an apology video saying he's "not perfect".

Carrington Franklin-Nakwaasah

Carrington, 32, is Kirk's adopted daughter.

Carrington, who was born in 1989, lives in Dallas, Texas with her family and works in branding and marketing.

She and her husband Maxx Nakwaasah, who married in 2016, welcomed their baby boy Shiloh in April 2020.

Kennedy Franklin

Kennedy, who was born in 1997, is Tammy's and Kirk's daughter.

According to her Instagram profile, she is a member of the Baylor University co-ed cheerleading team.

She defended her dad over the video of an argument, shared by her brother Kerrion.
