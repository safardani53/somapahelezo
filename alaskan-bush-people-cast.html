Details On Alaskan Bush People's Cast and Their Net Worth

Alaskan Bush People is a docudrama-style reality television series based in Alaska. It follows the Brown family as they strive to survive in the woods while cut off from modern society.

It has been a long-running reality TV series, spanning multiple seasons with varying episode counts.

The show premiered its first season with 5 episodes from May 6, 2014, to June 15, 2014.

Subsequently, it continued to captivate audiences season after season, with season 14, the most recent one, comprising 11 episodes that aired from October 2, 2022, to December 5, 2022.

Throughout its history, the series has offered viewers a glimpse into the challenging and adventurous lifestyle of the Brown family as they navigate the Alaskan wilderness.

Alaskan Bush People has a 6.0 rating out of 10 on IMDb.

Know ‘Alaskan Bush People’ more closely

With the show’s widespread popularity, fans want to find out more about the cast members in depth.

So, let’s proceed and explore further details about them.

Billy Brown

Billy Brown, the star of the reality show Alaskan Bush People, died in February 2021 at the age of 68 after suffering a fatal seizure while on top of North Star Ranch.

He had already been in and out of the hospital for several years due to heart difficulties.

Billy Brown in the frame (Source: Deadline)

The physicians even warned Billy not to stay at such a high altitude because it was harmful to his health. Despite the advice, he preferred to enjoy his life in the mountains with his family. 

Billy’s net worth was $6 million before his death in February 2021.

Ami Brown

Ami Brown is a reality television star who was born on August 28, 1963, in Texas, USA.

She was formerly married to Billy. Ami married at the age of 15, and her husband, Billy, was 26 at the time.

Ami was diagnosed with Stage 4 lung cancer, an advanced condition with the potential to metastasize throughout her body.

Despite the largely discouraging prognoses, Ami remained resolute in her determination to battle the illness, regardless of the potential outcomes.

Ami Brown in the hospital (Source: Distractify)

She has miraculously survived cancer and is now focused on moving on with her life in the absence of Billy. Fans claimed on social media in March 2021 Ami was faking her cancer diagnosis for TV show ratings.

To put an end to the suspicions, Ami’s doctors confirmed her diagnosis and treatments in a statement to People.

Her plans for the future include building a new barn for her animals and expanding the size of her ranch.

She has managed to amass an impressive net worth of 12 million dollars throughout her career.

Matthew Brown

Matthew Brown, commonly known as Matt Brown, was born on September 7, 1983, in Texas. As the eldest son of the renowned Brown family, he carries a significant role within their adventurous clan.

Remarkably, Matt was just three years old when his parents embarked on an unforgettable journey, leaving behind the Lone Star State to set sail for the wilds of Alaska.

Since then, he has grown into a man of diverse experiences and is an integral part of the fascinating Brown family saga.

Despite the show’s excellent reviews and popularity, Matt hasn’t appeared on it in a while.

Matt was estranged from his parents due to substance abuse. In 2016, he attended rehab for alcoholism for the first time.

He stated, “I could see myself spiraling.” He also added, “I was more withdrawn. I was slower. Things didn’t excite me the way they used to.”

After 35 days in an inpatient treatment facility, Matt changed his “weakness into a strength.” He adds, “Not everyone makes it back, and I’m grateful to be one of those who did.”

When his brother Bear was asked about him, he replied, “Matt’s been doing his own thing.”

He also added, “I don’t really see him much these days. I hope he finds the peace and serenity he seeks in his life.”

Matt’s net worth is reportedly around 1 million dollars, according to various sources. However, it’s crucial to verify this estimate for accuracy and reliability.

Gabriel Brown

Gabriel Starbuck Brown, born on December 15, 1989, in Alaska, is affectionately known as Gabe. In 2019, he tied the knot with Raquell Rose and welcomed their first child in 2020, followed by a second in 2021.

Beyond his reality TV fame, Gabe has cultivated a unique social media presence, often sporting eyeliner as a form of self-expression, artistic exploration, or dealing with grief.

Furthermore, he receives mixed reactions from fans and family, but he remains true to himself and does what makes him happy.

Recently, Gabe posted his smiling picture with the caption, “Happy is my default, doesn’t mater if the sky’s falling I’m’a be smiling regardless.”

He also added, “There’s always something to be grateful for. God bless y’all.”

As of 2023, Gabe’s estimated net worth stands at $700,000.

Bear Brown

Bear Brown, originally named Solomon Isaiah Freedom Brown, was born on June 10, 1987, in Alaska, USA, as the third child of Billy and Ami.

He is the first child in the family to be born and raised in Alaska, where he acquired vital survival skills such as hunting, fishing, shelter-building, and adapting to rugged conditions.

Bear was called the King of Extreme due to his love of adventure and extreme sports.

He obtained his education alongside his brothers through homeschooling, with literature and nature serving as his major teachers.

Bear Brown, celebrating his mother’s 60th birthday with his family (Source: Instagram)

Furthermore, he never went to a regular school or college and instead learned through parental supervision and his natural interest.

Bear’s interests also included reading comic books and viewing movies on a generator-powered TV, where he dreamed of being a superhero or a movie star.

As of 2023, Bear’s estimated net worth is approximately $200,000.

Noah Brown

Noah Darkcloud Brown, born on July 18, 1992, in Alaska’s rough landscape, developed an early interest in all things mechanical.

Unlike his more outspoken siblings, Noah’s introverted personality drew him to books, where he found sanctuary.

Beyond his role as a cast member on Alaskan Bush People, Noah has worked on diverse television projects, showcasing his talents as an actor, writer, and costume designer.

He notably portrayed Orlando’s Son Vincent in a 2018 episode of The Family Business and contributed as both an actor and costume designer in a 2004 episode of Krui-TV.

Furthermore, he exhibited his creative prowess by writing and playing Detective O’Neil, Hacker, in four episodes of No Shame TV from 2012 to 2013.

Noah Brown’s anticipated net worth in 2023 is $300,000.

Joshua Brown

Joshua Bam Bam Brown, born on September 18, 1984, in Alaska, USA, is about to turn 39 on September 18, 2023.

As the second-oldest among seven siblings, he gained fame alongside his family while embarking on their off-the-grid wilderness journey. Standing at a height of 5 feet 8 inches (1.75 m), Joshua’s physical stature matches his adventurous spirit.

Joshua Brown posing for a picture (Source: IMDb)

Furthermore, he’s undergone a notable transformation by becoming more active on his Instagram account, sharing glimpses of his life with fans.

This shift from his private nature suggests a newfound connection with his audience.

With an estimated net worth of $600,000, Joshua continues to make his mark in both the wild and the digital world.

Bird Brown

Amora Jean Snowbird Brown, also known as Birdy, was born on November 18, 1994, in Alaska’s landscape.

This nature-lover is 28 years old in 2023 and now dwells at North Star Ranch in Tonasket, Washington. Her undying passion for animals and profound connection to nature characterize her essence.

Bird stands 5 feet 5 inches (165 cm) tall and weighs 50 kilograms (110 pounds).

Following the debut of Season 14 of the reality series Alaskan Bush People, Birdy underwent a surgical procedure to eliminate an 8-pound tumor located on her ovary.

Later, she reported a complete recovery from the surgery, enabling her to return to her usual routines, including tasks like lifting hay and carrying out household chores.

She also expressed her ability to participate in workouts and regain a sense of her usual self.

Currently, she has reunited with her family at North Star Ranch in Tonasket, Washington, and has resumed her role on the television program.

Her net worth as a reality TV personality is believed to be $100,000. 

Rain Brown

Rain is an inspiring writer and television personality from the United States. She was born on November 23, 2002, in Hoonah, Alaska, United States. 

Rain first appeared on television when she was about 11 years old. She is most known for appearing alongside her family in Alaskan Bush People, a Discovery Channel reality-documentary series.

Rain Brown with her mother (Source: Instagram)

She is 5 feet 9 inches (175 cm) tall and weighs 110 pounds (50 kg) despite her height.

Her well-balanced physique is complemented by dark brown hair that cascades gently, and she has piercing gray eyes.

Rain received most of her education through homeschooling during her early years, although she briefly experienced traditional public schooling.

She is currently studying business administration and aims to apply what she learns to her environmental activities.

On June 19, 2023, Rain shared a heartfelt Instagram video featuring various photos of herself and her father, expressing her deep affection with the caption,

“Happys das day to the worlds most incredible da you gave me the most amazing eighteen years, you were a teacher, a confidant a lover a preacher, and most of all a giver.”

She also added, “Fly high in the place made only for the greats. And a very happy das day to all you incredible das out there ♥️”

She has an estimated net worth of $200k, and her main source of income on Alaskan Bush People is from $40,000 to $60,000 per episode.

Asa Siegel

Asa Siegel is well-known for his roles in films and television shows such as Max Payne 3 in 2012, Sexchange in 2003, and the popular reality series Alaskan Bush People.

Asa plays a significant role as the narrator of the show, adding depth and perspective to the unique experiences of the Brown family in the Alaskan wilderness.

Despite their contributions to these projects, there is limited public information available about Asa.
