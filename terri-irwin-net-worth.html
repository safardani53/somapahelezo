Terri Irwin is the star of one of the rarest kind of TV shows.

Animal Planet’s Crikey! It’s the Irwins chronicles the life of the world-famous Irwin family as they deal with various challenges and struggles at the Australia Zoo.

Following the family’s patriarch Steve Irwin’s death, the Australia Zoo is managed by wife Terri.

Who is Steve Irwin’s wife Terri?

The American-Australian naturalist and author, Terri originally hails from Eugene, Oregon. Thanks to her reality shows, she has now become a household name.

She is the current owner of the Australia Zoo. The TV star forayed into the world of wildlife through her job as a veterinary technician, which she started in 1989.

It was during her 1991 Australia tour that she met her husband. The pair got married in 1992 and remained together till Steve’s death in 2006. They have two children together Bindi and Robert Clarence Irwin.

Over the years owning a successful and full blooming zoo and starring in a TV show might have considerably added to the Irwin family’s wealth.

What is Terri Irwin’s net worth?

Terri operated the Australia Zoo, located in Beerwah, Queensland, Australia with their husband Steve until he passed away in 2006.

The 56-year-old initially co-starred with husband Steve on the television series The Crocodile Hunter, Croc Files and The Crocodile Hunter Diaries.

In 2006, the millionaire reality star and naturalist was made an honorary member of the Order of Australia for her services to wildlife conservation and the tourism industry in the country.

Boyfriends and past relationships explored!

In a 2018 interview with People, Terri confessed that she wasn’t looking for love after her husband’s death.

She said,”I think it’s wonderful when people who have lost someone find love again, but I’m not personally looking, and I haven’t been on a date in 27 years.”

However, in the past, the Irwin show star’s longtime friendship with the actor Russell Crowe has sparked romance rumours in the tabloids. She has been linked to Paul and Hulk Hogan too. But the star has dismissed all these rumours.
