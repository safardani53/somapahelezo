Brooks Koepka's stunning fiancee Jena Sims strips naked for cheeky Instagram post to celebrate 33rd

BROOKS KOEPKA'S fiancee Jena Sims paraded her 33rd birthday suit in a naked Instagram shot.

There was no sign of a driver as the golf hero's partner leaned into an open-top car for the exotic pose.

Next to the sultry photo for her 242,000-plus followers, Jena joked: “Maybe this year I’ll find my car chapstick."

Former world No1 Koepka celebrated too on his own Instagram page.

Alongside a peach emoji he wrote: “Happy birthday Jena.. Big year ahead, can’t wait." Jena replied: “Love u boo.”

But it was her own classy Instagram pic, surrounded by sun-lit trees, that brought in the fans for the B-movie star.

Responses included "birthday suit ballin!" from Taylor Stern.

But Jena's model friend Shannon Ford surely won the lingo bingo with: “A lil birthday booty ya just can’t beat it."

Jena and fellow American Koepka, 31, announced their engagement in April after reportedly dating since 2017.

No wedding date has been revealed but four-time Major winner Koepka apparently has clear ideas about the arrangements.
