Celebrities Who've Dressed Up as Britney Spears

Britney Spears isn't considered a pop icon for nothing. The 41-year-old hitmaker has inspired a generation of people and she continues to do so today. Among her biggest fans? Some of the world's biggest superstars.

From Paris Hilton to Tyra Banks, see which celebrities have dressed up as the Princess of Pop over the years.

Paris Hilton

For the 2023 Casamigos Halloween Party which took place Oct. 27 in L.A., Paris Hilton paid homage to her legendary pal by donning the flight attendant costume from her "Toxic" music video. Her husband, Carter Reum, was the pilot.

Jessica Alba & Kelly Sawyer Patricof

Jessica Alba and her friend, Baby2Baby founder Kelly Sawyer Patricof, also channeled the Princess of Pop for the Casamigos Halloween bash this year.

The actress also opted for a "Toxic" inspired ensemble, wearing Spears' embellished nude bodysuit from the video, while Patricof went as Spears from the groundbreaking "...Baby One More Time" video.

Brie Larson

Another fan of Spears, Brie Larson also donned the school-girl 'fit from the Brit's 1998 hit one Halloween.

Tyra Banks

What can we say? "...Baby One More Time" is a pop classic for good reason.

Katy Perry and Riff Raff

For the 2014 MTV Video Music Awards, Katy Perry and Riff Raff pulled up wearing denim-on-denim-on-denim-on-denim, a nod to Spears and Justin Timberlake's unforgettable look from the 2001 American Music Awards

Devon Windsor and Jonathan Barbara

Devon Windsor and her beau, Jonathan Barbara, took on Spears and Timberlake for their Halloween getups back in 2018.

Rachel Hilbert

For her 2019 Halloween look, model Rachel Hilbert went extraterrestrial and channeled Britney Spears from the "Oops!...I Did It Again" music video.
