Who is in the Strictly Come Dancing 2023 final?

AFTER weeks of hard work, the finalists of Strictly Come Dancing 2023 are preparing to take to the dancefloor one last time.

From which notable names are in with a chance of winning the iconic Glitterball trophy to when the Strictly Come Dancing final will take place - here’s everything you need to know.

What happens in a Strictly Come Dancing final?

In the Strictly 2023 final the remaining couples will strut their stuff on the dance floor to be crowned champions and win the Glitterball trophy.

In a bid for the iconic trophy, the couples are taking on three terrific performances.

Claudia Winkleman and Tess Daily will be hosting, with Tess staying close to the dance floor to speak to the couples following each dance, and Claudia looking after the pairs in the gallery as they wait to take to the stage.

As always judges Shirley Ballas, Craig Revel Horwood, Motsi Mabuse and Anton Du Beke will give their scores, helping viewers to choose their winner.

The victors will be decided by a public vote.

There are a number of ways to vote for your champion — including online and over the telephone.

To vote online you need to head to the BBC website and log into your account.

Once you are logged in you can vote for your favourite up to three times — or you can spread your three votes out if you can't decide who you want to win.

You can also vote by phone by calling the number shown on screen throughout the live show.

With an average age of 23, this year's trio are the youngest line-up to make it to the last week of the BBC show.
