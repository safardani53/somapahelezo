So far in his career

Joakim began his football career with his local team Ostervra IF before moving to AaB at the age of 12. He broke into the first-team squad in 2016 at the age of 19, thanks to hard work and devotion, and earned a full-time deal with the club the following year. Later that year, he agreed to a new deal until 2020. He joined the Belgian club Genk in July 2017, following right-back Timothy Castagne. He did not start his debut season with the club because he was competing for a starting spot with Clinton Mata.

After Clinton Mata came to Club Brugge, he was able to secure a permanent starting spot in his second season. With three goals and five assists, he helped the squad capture their fourth league championship. Atlanta signed him to a five-year deal in December 2020, but his move was not finalized until January 4, 2021. In 25 appearances for his nation, Joakim has nine goals. He has grown to fame as a result of his remarkable style of play and is often recognized as one of the best defenders in the world. Many large teams, like West Ham United and Tottenham Hotspur, have shown interest in him.

He praises his parents for setting the road for his career.

Joakim was born in Ostervra to his parents, Ole and Annemette. He began playing football with his brothers at a young age. In one interview, he said that he and his brother were crushed on the road. Ole, Joakim’s father, was a legend for Ostervra’s first squad. He coached Joakim for numerous years. He still seeks and listens to his father’s counsel. He attributes his success to his parents, as well as his siblings and childhood friends.

As a player, Joakim

As a participant Joakim sees himself as a highly energetic athlete who strives to give his all for his teams and fans. He describes himself as an offensive-minded fullback who can play on both flanks. He is a really pleasant guy who wants the best for everyone, according to Joakim. He values his family and enjoys spending time with them.  Joakim wants to grow as a player and as a person. He wants to grow with the team and contribute to its success.

He would have become a sports instructor if he hadn’t been a player.

When asked what he’d do if he wasn’t a player, he responded he’d probably be a teacher. He will be teaching sports to the younger generation and informing them about the sport and an active lifestyle.

His finest and worst qualities

One of his strongest attributes, as highlighted by Joakim, is his ability to keep trying without giving up. When asked about his weaknesses, he claims he is quite sloppy when it comes to being on time. Some people like structure, but he prefers to take things day by day.
