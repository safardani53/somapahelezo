Lindsay Lohan Playboy Pictures Hit the Internet

First, the cover. Now, the entire spread.

A couple days after the Lindsay Lohan Playboy cover leaked online, the same fate has befallen a slew of photos from this magazine’s January/February issue. They depict the “actress” doing her best Marilyn Monroe impression… naked, of course.

Dina Lohan has said her daughter’s spread will be “tastefully done” and, having seen the photos, we can say this is kind of true, if you consider a lack of full frontal nudity to be “tasteful.”

But Lindsay – who reportedly made $1 million for the day of work – is most definitely nude in each shot, often exposing her breasts and a decent amount of crack.

“Someone took photos of Lindsay’s pictorial in an advanced copy of the January/February issue,” a Playboy rep said. “They posted the photos online without permission. We are not releasing any official images or information until Dec. 15.”

We’ll publish the Lindsay Lohan photos on THG then. Yes, we love showing you Lindsay’s naughty parts – but we love not getting sued even more.
