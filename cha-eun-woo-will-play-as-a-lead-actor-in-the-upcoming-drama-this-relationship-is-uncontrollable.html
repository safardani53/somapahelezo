What has Cha Eun-woo been up to lately?

The ASTRO star just completed shooting for his next drama Island, in which he co-stars with Kim Nam-gil, Lee Da-hee, and Sung-Joon. Island is a fantasy exorcism drama set on Jeju Island. It recounts the painful path of people who are selected to confront evil and save the world. The drama will be directed by Bae Jong, well known for his work on Welcome to Dongmakgol and Fabricated City. The ASTRO singer will portray John, a hip Catholic exorcist and priest with the noble mission of safeguarding lives and purging evil.

Cha Eun-woo made his acting debut in the drama Hit the Top and went on to achieve success with My ID is Gangnam Beauty. He has previously been in a number of successful projects, including Rookie Historian Goo Hae-ryung and True Beauty, in which he co-starred with Moon Ga-young and Hwang In-yeop. The ASTRO singer is now amid fan meetups around Asia. He just returned from a fan meeting in Thailand and is set to visit the Philippines shortly.
