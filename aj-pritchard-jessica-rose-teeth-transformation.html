AJ Pritchard and Jessica Lever reveal epic teeth transformations

AJ Pritchard and Jessica Rose Lever have revealed their epic teeth transformations.

The professional ballroom dancer, 26, and reality star, 27, showed off their new set of pearly whites.

Since last year Dr Richard Marques has teamed up with Diamond Whites to help everyone achieve perfect teeth without going to the dentist.

Now stars including AJ and and Jessica have decided give it a try and looked very happy with the results as they posed with the Diamond Whites pack.

The choreographer wore a casual blue T-shirt and jeans as he was snapped holding his teeth aligner on a beige staircase.

Jess Lever - formerly Jess Shears, who appeared on Love Island in 2017 - donned a white shirt and white top underneath as she smiled for the camera with the Diamond Whites kit in her right hand.

The snaps were posted on Diamond White's Instagram account, alongside a caption which read: "Join @aj11ace and thousands of others starting their teeth straightening journey with Diamond Whites Invisible Aligners.

"Book a Home Impression Kit or Digital Scan at any of our 30 locations nationwide."

They wrote on another post: "@jessica_rose_uk is on her journey to straighter teeth with #DiamondWhites and we can’t wait to see the transformation."

The starter pack includes a Home Impression Kit, Free teeth whitening kit, 3D before and after visuals from £99.
