Naked photos of I'm A Celeb's Jack Maynard leaked online in latest scandal to hit YouTuber

FULL frontal photos of controversial I’m A Celeb star Jack Maynard have been leaked online.

A pair of nude snaps showing the 22-year-old campmate posing with his pants down have emerged online in a second scandal to hit the You Tuber in as many days.

One of the explicit images taken on Snapchat showed Jack grinning with nothing but a hat on in a bedroom as he gave the camera a thumbs up.

It was believed to have been shared as a joke between his mates before making its way into the public domain.

In another, he was seen in a bathroom with his jogging bottoms around his knees as he captured the moment on his camera phone.

It comes after Jack, the brother of pop star Conor Maynard, 24, was forced to apologise for making racist and homophobic slurs on Twitter which had left his future on the ITV jungle show in doubt on Monday night.

I'm A Celebrity 2017 jungle secrets revealed

The Sun revealed that on his official Twitter account he casually used the N-word and branded other users “retarded” and “f****ts”.

In tweets dating back up to six years Jack, followed by 650,000 impressionable young fans, mocked gay men, called his friends the racist term and laughed at people with facial disfigurements.

As the ITV show launched to millions, Jack admitted the words he used were “inflammatory” and “insulting”.

A spokeswoman for Jack said: “Jack is ashamed of these tweets, many were deleted a long time ago.”
