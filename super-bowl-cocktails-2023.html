Drinks &amp; Recipes For Watching The Big Game Hollywood Life

While the Super Bowl, like most sporting events, is associated with beer, there are plenty of other ways to get into the ‘spirit’ of the Big Game. When the Kansas City Chiefs meet the Philadelphia Eagles in Glendale, Arizona, for Super Bowl LVII, you better have something cool and refreshing on-hand, because these evenly-matched teams are going to crank up the heat for what promises to be a great game.

We’ve provided some suggestions for cocktails to make with what you have in your liquor cabinet (or what you can find via Drizly, Uber Eats, and other delivery apps.) This year is one to try something new because such is the spirit of Super Bowl LVII. The Big Game see an influx of different spirits brands all advertising in 2023. Rémy Martin, Crown Royal, and other liquor brands will pony up a record-high average of $7 million for a 30-second commercial. This is because Anheuser-Busch, the parent company for Budweiser, Michelob Ultra, and Busch brand beers, has ended its exclusivity deal with the Super Bowl, a deal first made in 1989. Molson Coors, Heineken, and others will advertise, alongside Bud, Michelob, and Busch.

However, Heineken will advertise its Heineken 0.0, it’s alcohol-free beer. Because even sober people (or those whose Dry January has extended into February) enjoy the Super Bowl as well. Some of the following recipes can be made without alcohol, in case someone wants something other than soda, water, or coffee to drink during the game – you do you.
