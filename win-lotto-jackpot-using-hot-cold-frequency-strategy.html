I'm a lottery expert - the simple Frequency strategy using hot and cold numbers can land you million

THERE is an easy way to hit the lotto jackpot with this Frequency strategy using hot and cold numbers.

When a number is hot, it means that it commonly appeared in the past few lottery drawings.

Cold numbers are less common to pop up in a current draw.

There are pros and cons to both kinds of numbers, which is why using both hot and cold numbers may increase your chances of winning big.

Hot numbers may have a better chance of appearing in the draw or they may not because they have been too frequent.

While cold numbers have a smaller probability of appearing, they may start to pop up because it's been a while since they've been drawn.

Some people choose to pick overdue numbers which have not been drawn for quite some time so that the pattern can be reversed, Lottery Texts revealed.

It is important to note that hot numbers are different from the most common lottery-winning numbers.

Numbers can be commonly drawn as bonus balls or for second-chance draws, but hot numbers are the ones that will win you the jackpot.

So, try to play both hot and cold numbers, even some overdue numbers, as the mix of high and low probabilities might land you millions.
