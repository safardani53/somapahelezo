REASON TO SMILE

Jenelle shared even more footage from her fun night out on social media, ignoring the negative comments.

She posted a video to her Instagram Story with the caption: "#HappyBirthday to me! 32 years young."

The second half of the video showed Jenelle dressed up in a white sweater with matching faux fur around the wrists and collar.

She pushed her straightened, sleek hair back with a headband.

The mom of three completed the look with a bold red lip.

David also took to Instagram to wish her a happy birthday.

SERIOUS ALLEGATIONS

The birthday bash comes amid claims as Jenelle has faced backlash for continuing to stand by David after Jace accused him of assaulting him outside a neighbor's North Carolina home in September.

He allegedly attacked Jace before the boy ran away for a third time.

David "willingly harmed" his stepson, and left marks on his arm and neck, according to court documents first revealed by The U.S. Sun.

The court filing stated: "The defendant unlawfully and willingly did, being the parent of Jace V Evans, who was a child, less than 16 years of age, inflict physical injury on that child.

"The physical injury inflicted caused marks on their right arm and left and right side of the neck, and was inflicted by other than accidental means."

David was charged with child abuse in late October.

He had a court appearance regarding the charges on November 29th but did not appear.

A local attorney appeared on his behalf and requested a continuance as the attorney David hired is on maternity leave until January.

The judge allowed the continuance and set another court date for January 18th.

Jenelle has defended her husband several times since he was accused of child abuse.

Shortly after criminal charges were raised against him, she released a statement to The DailyMail that said: "The police are looking into the wrong person right now. There is a vile situation going on and it’s not pertaining to my husband.
