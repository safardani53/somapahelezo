Shoppers rush to Tesco for 'gorgeous' 25 teddy coat that will 'keep you so warm' &amp; it has fleecy p

A FASHION fanatic has revealed she’s found the perfect coat to keep you warm this winter - and it’s only £25 from Tesco.

Ellie Butler regularly shares her shopping hauls and outfit creations with her army of followers on social media.

In a recent video, she bagged some new cosy buys and partywear pieces from the supermarket's F&F range.

Ellie - the girlfriend of Celtic's Liam Shaw, currently on loan at Wigan Athletic - said: "I've just been to do my food shop and you will not believe the clothes I found in Tesco. 

"I've never really looked at the clothes in Tesco before. it's never really crossed my mind.

"Then I'm walking around, walking around. I've spent £400 in Tesco on clothes."

She added: "Run to Tesco because I cannot believe my eyes. I'm still in shock."

And Ellie was stunned to find a “gorgeous” teddy coat scanning for half the price of its original £50 tag. 

The cream coat even has fleecy lined pockets to keep your hands warm.

Ellie said: "When I saw what the price was I couldn't believe it."So this was half price and there were loads of stock at my Tesco so hopefully your local Tesco will have the same as well.

"Should have been £50, it was £25.

"And it's this big teddy coat [in] a gorgeous beigy colour. It's got the black buttons, it's so heavy, so thick.”

Trying it on at home, she added: "Obsessed with this. I think this was a large so it's perfectly oversized. I can pop hoodies underneath. 

"I feel like this will keep you so warm and the pockets are also lined with the same material as well so your hands are going to be so nice and warm as well. 

"For £25, if they have this in your Tesco - honestly I'm praying they do because I can't believe it's half price.

"I don't know why they'd make it this reduced in winter when everyone's buying coats.

"But yeah definitely go get yourself a bargain if you're nearby a Tesco because this is gorgeous."

And her fellow fashion fans are also loving the bargain buy.

One said: “I went and bought the half price coat, it is amazing. Really happy with.”

“Omg F&F is killing it”, a second gushed.

Meanwhile, a third wrote: “As if you got that cutie from Tesco, wow. Guess where I'm going tomorrow.”

Elsewhere in her hail, Ellie bagged the £55 dupe of Zara’s viral faux fur coat. 

"Can't believe this is from Tesco F&F", she gushed.

"This speaks for itself. The Zara coat, but the Tesco version and I actually prefer this.

"It's so cosy and it's so warm. I think obsessed would be an understatement for this, it is perfect."
