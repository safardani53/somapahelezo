Kushal Tandon Biography

Kushal Tandon is an Indian model turned actor popular on television. Kushal is known for his role of Rahul Nanda in the Alt Balaji web series (2018).]

Kushal Tandon was born on , in Kanpur, India to Sandhya and Billu Tandon. His father is in the field of transport and oil business and is also the President of Transport Union in UP. He has two elder sisters named Tina Bhushan and Bhavana Tandon Kapoor.
