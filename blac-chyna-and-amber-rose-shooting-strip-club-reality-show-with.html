Shooting Strip Club Reality Show with Tyga and Scott Disick?

It was only a matter of time before reality TV tackled this genre.

Back in November, TMZ reported that West Hollywood gentlemen’s club, Ace of Diamonds would get its own reality show, focusing on the lives of its employees (strippers), and the celebrities that frequent the establishment.

Radar Online claims that Blac Chyna and Amber Rose are tied to the project, which makes sense given that they’re constantly photographed arriving at the club.  

Both women are former strippers, so portraying Ace of Diamonds’ ladies in a different light is likely something Chyna and Rose would support.

“Blac Chyna and Amber are currently filming a pilot that centers around the strip club, and it is being pitched to a variety of networks," a source told Radar.

"The reason that all of these Kardashian-related celebs have been going there is because they are all getting paid to appear in the pilot to make it more interesting than it already is.”

Two of those "Kardashian-related" celebs are Chyna’s ex, Tyga and Scott Disick.

"Blac Chyna and Amber know that having a reality show based on them working at a high-priced strip club near Beverly Hills is guaranteed to make ratings and they are right,” the source added.

“Now they just have to find a taker. Look out Kris Jenner!”

Ace of Diamonds is a block away from Lisa Vanderpump’s restaurants, Sur and Pump, so let’s add fuel to the fire and assume a crossover episode with Vanderpump Rules will happen.
