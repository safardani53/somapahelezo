The 65 Best Advent Calendars of 2023

Advent calendars are one of our favorite ways to get into the holiday spirit. Whether we’re counting down the days until December 25, celebrating the 12 Days of Christmas, or celebrating the eight days of Hanukkah, these festive calendars are a fun holiday tradition with no shortage of nostalgia. 

With December fast approaching, now is the best time to buy an advent calendar for the 2023 holiday season before they sell out. Since there are so many options available — and the holiday season is hectic enough — we did the research so you can spend more time relaxing, spending time with family, and enjoying the festivities.

From traditional chocolate ones to kid advent calendars (including the oh-so-popular Lego advent calendars) to beauty gift sets, we share the best advent calendars for Christmas, plus the best Hanukkah calendars, too, ahead. 

Best Chocolate Advent Calendars

Crate & Barrel Chocolate Advent Calendar

The Crate & Barrel Chocolate Advent Calendar features a green triangle-shaped box inspired by a Christmas tree and decked out in ball ornaments. Only these ornaments are actually caramel, white peppermint, milk, and dark chocolate truffles wrapped in foil papers for extra sparkle, putting an elevated spin on the classic chocolate advent calendar tradition. 

Godiva Classic Chocolate Advent Calendar

If you love Godiva chocolate, you don’t want to miss out on this year’s limited edition Godiva advent calendar. The Holiday Snowglobe Advent Calendar boasts a traditional design with a snowglobe illustration on the front of the chocolate box and opens up to reveal a festive Christmas tree with 24 proliferated boxes featuring cherry, mint, caramel, ganache, caramel, coconut, and solid chocolate squares inside.  

Williams Sonoma Peppermint Bark Advent Calendar

Elevate your chocolate advent calendar tradition with this delicious Peppermint Bark Advent Calendar from Williams Sonoma. The adorable advent calendar comes in a festive red box with vintage-inspired lettering and features 24 peppermint bark chocolates shaped like Christmas trees, Santa, toy soldiers, and snowmen.  

Sugarfina Santa’s Candy Shop Advent Calendar

For the candy lover, consider the Sugarfina Advent Calendar, inspired by Santa’s candy shop. This calendar is a cross between a chocolate box and an advent calendar and features a mix of the brand’s most beloved gourmet holiday sweets, including chocolates and gummies.  

Harry & David Advent Cookie Calendar

If chocolate isn’t your thing, but you still want to indulge in a daily sweet treat this holiday season, consider the Harry & David Advent Cookie Calendar. This edible advent calendar features a collection of Harry & David’s top holiday cookies, including classics like gingerbread sandwich cookies and raspberry shortbread cookies, as well as double chocolate cherry cookies and dark chocolate peppermint cookies. 

Jelly Belly Harry Potter Jelly Bean Trivia Advent Calendar

World Market has some of the best advent calendars, and the Jelly Belly Harry Potter Jelly Bean Trivia Advent Calendar is one of our favorites. This candy advent calendar features gourmet jelly beans in various flavors, plus Hogwarts trivia to help you count down until Christmas. 

Best Food Advent Calendars

Uncommon Goods 12 Days of Hot Sauce Advent Calendar

Calling all hot sauce lovers — this advent calendar is for you. The Uncommon Goods 12 Days of Hot Sauce Advent Calendar features 12 perforated boxes with spicy surprises hidden inside. Inspired by a classic advent calendar, the box includes a mix of mini hot sauce bottles ranging from sweet to savory.  

Williams Sonoma Holiday Advent Calendar: 24 Days of Baking Cookies

This adorable 24 Days of Baking Cookies advent calendar from Williams Sonoma is made specifically with bakers in mind. The adorable food advent calendar features a collection of cookie-making tools, including a spatula, sugar shaker, stencils, and—of course—biscuit and cookie cutters, too. 

Whirley Pop 6-Qt. Popcorn Popper & Advent Calendar

If you love the crunch of a good holiday popcorn, then you’ll adore the Whirley Pop 6-Qt. Popcorn Popper & Advent Calendar set from Sur La Table. This Christmas tree-shaped advent calendar features 24 popcorn-themed treats, including kernels, seasonings, and sweets for all of your snacking pleasures. Plus, it comes with a popcorn popper so you can create your own blends.  

Bonne Maman Limited Edition Advent Calendar

For jelly, jams, and honey lovers, the Bonne Maman 2023 Limited Edition Advent Calendar is a must. Inspired by the classic cut-out advent calendars, this set comes with 24 mini spread jars, including preserves, honey, and jelly for your morning breakfast. 

Maker Wine 12 Days of Canned Wine Advent Calendar

Sip your way into the holiday season with the Maker Wine Advent Calendar. This alcohol advent calendar features 12 cans of wine—which is equal to about four bottles of wine—and comes with a pairings guide to take your holiday countdown to the next level. 

English Tea Shop Organic Tea Advent Calendar

The World Market English Tea Shop Organic Tea Advent Calendar is the best advent calendar for tea lovers. This advent calendar is inspired by the traditional countdown boxes and features a variety of holiday tea flavors to help you count down the days until Christmas.

Uncommon Goods Merry Mixology Scratch-Off Advent Calendar

If you’re looking for the best advent calendar for an at-home mixologist, consider the Uncommon Goods Merry Mixology Scratch-Off Calendar. This advent calendar is unique in that it doesn’t actually feature cocktails and instead has 12 scratch-off QR codes that take you to virtual cocktail-making classes. Learn how to make everything from a New York whiskey sour and classic hot toddy to a festive spicy paloma and a gingerbread old-fashioned.  

Woodford Reserve 8 Day Cocktail Advent Calendar

The Woodford Reserve 8-Day Cocktail Advent Calendar is an excellent choice for cocktail lovers. This festive advent calendar features everything you need to make creative cocktails, including cocktail mixers and several types of bitters. 

Keurig 24 Days of Cheer Advent Calendar

Coffee drinkers can kick off the holiday season with a cozy cup of joe, thanks to the Keurig 24 Cups of Cheer Advent Calendar. This coffee advent calendar features some of Keurig’s most popular coffee blends from McCafe, Peet’s, Krispy Kreme, and Cinnabon (to name a few).

Meri Meri has some of the best wooden advent calendars, and the Dinosaur Advent Calendar is another one of our favorites. This kids’ advent calendar features everything you need to create a prehistoric scene, including foliage, dinosaurs, and a cardboard backdrop. 

Lego Star Wars Advent Calendar

Kids and adults alike will enjoy this Lego advent calendar featuring their favorite Star Wars characters. The advent calendar comes with 24 miniature Lego gifts and includes a mix of characters — including an Ewok, Princess Leia, and Gonk Droid dressed as a reindeer — and vehicles like the Imperial Star Destroyer and Mandalorian's N-1 Starfighter.

Disney Classic Advent Calendar

The Disney Classic Advent Calendar is another fun and festive kids’ advent calendar for the season. This one features 32 figurines, decorations, and stickers to help kick off the holiday season. Plus, the decorations and stickers make for great interactive play. 

Polly Pocket Dolls Advent Calendar, Gingerbread House Playset
