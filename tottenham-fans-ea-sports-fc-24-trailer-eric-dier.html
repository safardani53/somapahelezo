Eagle-eyed Tottenham fans say 'how has he finessed his way in?' as they spot shock player in EA Spor

EAGLE-EYED Tottenham Hotspur fans have asked "how has he finessed his way in?" after spotting Eric Dier in the new EA Sports FC 24 trailer.

EA Sports released the first trailer for their latest football video game on Monday.

The video features a host of the biggest stars from the world's game, appearing as their in-game likenesses.

EA Sports FC 24 will be the first release since the end of the developer's relationship with FIFA.

However, Spurs fans were more interested in Dier's appearance in the trailer.

He appears in a changing room alongside Jude Bellingham and Andy Robertson.
