McDonald's Squishmallows Happy Meals return and US fans can't wait to get their hands on adorable to

MCDONALD'S has announced Happy Meals will come with Squishmallows plushies.

There are six limited edition, Halloween-themed plushies available for fans to collect.

The toys are available in the UK until October 31, but are not currently available in America.

Customers in the United States can collect Pokemon toys with their food until the Squishmallows arrive later this year.

According to rumors, the viral plushies will be in American Happy Meals in December.

Squishmallow fans are flocking to McDonald's to get their hands on the special-edition toys and sharing what they get on social media.

What Squishmallows are included?

The limited-edition plushies are:

  • Bella the spider
  • Patricio the bat
  • Brock the batdog
  • Stump the skeleton cat
  • Wade the werewolf
  • Autumn the cat

Giant versions of the Squishmallows will also be appearing in McDonald's in the UK.

Customers can donate to Children in Need at the stores by entering a raffle to win the extra-large plushies.

There is no indication whether or not the giant editions will be available in the United States later this year.

Squishmallows will continue to be available in Happy Meals following the Halloween season. Fall themed toys will be available from November 1 to November 23.

It is unclear if the fall-themed toys will make their way to America.
