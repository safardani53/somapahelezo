What is Timothe Chalamet's net worth?

TIMOTHÉE Chalamet shot to fame as a popular actor, model, and partner of Kylie Jenner.

Hollywood star Timothée undoubtedly has a stable income from his numerous movie roles, but what is his total net worth?

What is Timothée Chalamet's net worth?

Internationally acclaimed actor Timothée Chalamet is considered a top pick for some casting directors in the film industry.

The young actor's net worth is supposedly on the rise between his film roles, modeling deals, and collaborations with luxury brands.

During his childhood, commercials and supporting roles meant he had already begun to amass massive earnings.

Timothée's feature film debut came in late 2014 with Men, Women And Children, where he played a small role as Danny Vance.

In October of the same year, the actor played a young Tom Cooper in Christopher Nolan's Interstellar.

Later, in 2018, he received a Best Actor Oscar nomination for Call Me By Your Name, which catapulted him to stardom.

The 27-year-old went on to have memorable roles in Lady Bird, Beautiful Boy and Woody Allen's A Rainy Day In New York - all of which reaffirmed his talent.

Meanwhile, on the red carpet the fashion icon won fans with unique looks and caught the attention of several luxury brands, with whom he went on to land lucrative endorsements.

Chalamet became the face of Chanel's men's perfume line Bleu de Chanel in May 2023.

As of November 2023, the star has a net worth of over $25 million, per Celebrity Net Worth.

How much did Timothée Chalamet get paid for Dune?

The French-American actor saw a turning point in his career after he landed a leading role in Dune back in 2021.

Directed by Denis Villeneuve, the fiction film reportedly grossed over $402 million and was critically acclaimed.

The blockbuster nabbed six out of ten Academy Awards, five BAFTA awards, and one Golden Globe.

Chalamet played the role of Paul Atreides, a young man living on the fictional planet Arrakis with his family.

Although there are no official reports on how much he made, Cosmopolitan points to rumors that he could have gotten around $2.2 million for the role.

Does Timothée Chalamet own a house?

Timothée is another celebrity who has dipped his feet into the world of real estate.

The star supposedly bought a mansion previously owned by model Kate Upton and her baseball star husband, Justin Verlander.

The West Coast property sits on 1.5 acres of land with approximately 5,521 square feet of living space.
