Marnie Simpson will marry Casey Johnson on TV for new series of Geordie Shore

GEORDIE Shore OG Marnie Simpson will marry her fiancé Casey Johnson on the show next series.

The stunning 31-year-old star, who shares two adorable children with Casey, shot to fame when she joined the cast of Geordie Shore in 2013, and now she’s going to share a very special moment on the show.

Geordie Shore will return for The Reunion series again this year – despite bosses saying the initial return was a one-off special.

Discussing Marnie’s wedding, a source told The Sun that it’s set to take place in March.

They said: “Marnie and Casey’s wedding is fast approaching and they’ve decided to finally tie the knot.

“Marnie is nervous but so excited and the wedding will happen when she returns from Colombia.”

The Sun contacted Marnie's representatives who had no comment.

The wedding will then be showcased on the upcoming series 24 of Geordie Shore.

They continued: “It’s set to be an intimate and meaningful ceremony which will take place in the UK.

“Only close friends and family will be in attendance and Marnie’s so happy that she’ll be able to share this moment with her co-stars too.”

Casey and Marnie met on the show Single AF in 2017.

They confirmed their relationship just a few months after the show ended - a move that is against show rules but it didn't seem to worry the smitten love-birds.

They then got engaged in August 2020 after Casey got down on one knee.

It came after they headed off on a date night together where he proposed after taking her to an orchard decorated with fairy lights.

She cooed: "Oooh, I feel like I'm in Narnia" in an Instagram video in the idyllic setting before he popped the question off-camera.

They welcomed their first son, Rox, on October 29 2019, and then a second baby boy, Oax, on 18 May 2022.
