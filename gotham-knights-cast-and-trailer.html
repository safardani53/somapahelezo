Gotham Knights: Cast and trailer

ON MAY 31, 2022, The CW network released the official trailer for Gotham Knights.

Gotham Knights is a new series from The CW network.

Who is in the cast of Gotham Knights?

The CW's Gotham Knights cast is set to bring new faces to the small screen.

Some cast members include Misha Collins as Harvey Dent, Navia Robinson as Carrie Kelley, and Olivia Rose Keegan as Duela.

Oscar Morgan is set to play Turner Hayes with Rahart Adams as Brody.

Anna Lore will also star in the series as the character Stephanie, with costar Fallon Smythe as Harper Row.

Gotham Knights are based on DC Comics characters.

The new series was created by James Stoteraux, Natalie Abrams, and Chad Fiveash for The CW.

It is inspired by the story of Batman and will see a new twist to the story.

The cast includes members and supporting roles from the Batman family.

When will Gotham Knights premiere?

The official trailer for the show can be watched above this story.

While the trailer was also shared by the network on YouTube on May 31, the new series is not set to premiere until 2023.

Gotham Knights' official trailer is just over three-minutes long and includes the full cast.

The trailer starts with a party at the lavish home of Batman himself.

His son's party comes to a halt with a knock on the door from the cops.

They arrive to deliver the news of Batman's death.

What is Gotham Knights about?

Gotham Knights is a look at the world of Batman following the death of Bruce Wayne.

Bob Kane and Bill Finger are the authors of the original DC comic that the series is based on.
