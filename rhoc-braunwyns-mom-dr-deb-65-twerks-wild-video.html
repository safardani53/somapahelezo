RHOC star Braunwyn Windham-Burke's mom Dr. Deb, 65, twerks in a bikini in wild new video

REAL Housewives of Orange County star Braunwyn Windham-Burke's mother Doctor Deb, twerked in a bikini in a wild new Instagram video.

Dr. Deborah Windham, most commonly known as Dr. Deb, shared the video to her Instagram feed.

The clip showed the grandmother, 65, dressed in a skimpy bikini, with her colorful dreads shaking as she twerked to hippie house music in her living room.

Dr. Deb is the mother of Real Housewive's star Braunwyn, 42, and has appeared on seasons 14 and 15 of the Orange County edition several times.

She has been a wild addition to the show, bringing with her color and lots of personality.

When asked about her mother in August of 2019, Braunwyn told Entertainment Tonight: "My mom is fun! She is big and bright and colorful and, yeah, she's a big part of the show."

About a month later though, the blonde mom of seven expanded on her relationship with her mother, adding that it was more complicated than at first glance.

She told Bravo's The Daily Dish: "My relationship with my mom is more complicated than she realizes. I do know though that parents and children often have very different recollections of things and that I’m only seeing things from my point of view."

"I love my mom so much and really respect the fact that, as a single mom, she supported us and worked hard to become a doctor" she continues.

The eccentric grandmother has had quite the past, from being a "retired rock star" to going to medical school and becoming a practicing physician for over 25 years.

The colorful 65-year-old is also an avid Burning Man fan, frequenting the festival with her husband, endovascular neurosurgeon Brian Herman who is eight years her junior.

In a post shared from the festival last year, the mother of three jokingly compared Real Housewives to Burning Man, saying: "Experimenting for the next week... Burning Man, an experiment in temporary community, with a lot more camp drama than the Real Housewives!

"We’re doing a new series called Real housewives of Black Rock City."

Dr. Deb and her daughter's relationship issues have been exhibited on the Bravo show in the past.

During Braunwyn's vow renewal to husband Sean Burke of 30 years, Dr. Deb made some jarring comments in regards to her daughter's sobriety.

The MD of family medicine claimed that her daughter is "more boring" now that she has chosen a life without drinking.

Although Dr. Deb then tried to play off the comment, Braunwyn was visibly hurt from her mother's criticism.

Later, the reality TV star spoke out about the moment, saying: "My mom has been using her addiction as a weapon for so long.” 

Fans immediately took to Twitter after the episode to combat Deb's critical comments to Braunwyn and defend her choice to abstain from alcohol.

One angered fan wrote: "Braunwyn's mom is THE WORST. Broke down wannabe Betsy Johnson..."

Another questioned Dr. Deb's medical history, commenting: "I’ve said it before, I’ll say it again. @braunwyn your mom is no doctor.

"She’s walking, talking poison. Your sobriety will depend greatly on your ability to get out from under the heavy blanket of her opinion and judgment - and to never feel bad about doing so."

Braunwyn has been very open about her battle with alcoholism, even stating that she had seven kids in order to avoid the substance for years.

In a recent episode, the mom was joined by cast member Gina Kirschenheiter, who asked: "Do you think that that's why you continued to have babies?"
