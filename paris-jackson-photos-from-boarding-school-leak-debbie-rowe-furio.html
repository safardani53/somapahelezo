Paris Jackson Photos From Boarding School Leak, Debbie Rowe FURIOUS

Debbie Rowe is livid that someone sold photos of her troubled daughter, Paris Jackson, at the boarding school where she’s been for the last month.

She plans to sue the people who did so, according to TMZ.

Someone at the school, located outside California, has not just sold pictures but information about the teenager, who had been going there on the DL.

But the photos plastered on Twitter, such as the one above, were taken during a graduation ceremony, shortly after Paris arrived, blowing her cover.

She’s not even using her real name at the school.

 

In any case, Debbie Rowe is beyond pissed.

She’s telling friends that the people selling the Paris Jackson photos, and the websites that run them, are messing up her daughter’s ability to get better.

Rowe reportedly WANTS this particular story published, however, so people at the school and beyond know she’s upset and willing to take legal action.

Paris attempted suicide in June, taking pills and cutting her arm with a meat cleaver, leading to a prolonged hospitalization and treatment period.

We wish her nothing but the best and think that Debbie’s doing a terrific job as a mom, stepping in and protecting her daughter as best she can.
