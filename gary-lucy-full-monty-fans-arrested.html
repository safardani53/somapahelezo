The Full Monty's Gary Lucy reveals rowdy fans have been ARRESTED after watching him strip on stage

HE’S back on the market for the first time in 15 years, but Hollyoaks star Gary Lucy seems to be terrified, rather than flattered by his rabid admirers on The Full Monty tour.

The soap legend revealed fans have been whipped into such a frenzy watching him strip naked that they’ve begged him for private sessions in his dressing room and even been arrested while attempting to get close to him.

Asked if audience members ever cross the line, he said: “Yeah, all the time. How about, ‘can we become your fluffer on the road?’ ‘Can we bring our friend whose birthday is it into your dressing room and meet you personally?’

“Last week we were in Glasgow and we had four people arrested, thrown out the theatre and nicked. I think that’s probably the most raucous we’ve had.

“They just go absolutely ballistic from the minute the curtain goes up. You’re like, ‘come on you’ve got two hours yet, just chill’.

“You’ve got the theatre-goers sat next to them and they’re telling them to be quiet and they’re drunk and there’s a punch. It is what it is.”

I revealed Gary had split from wife Natasha in November, four years after their star-studded wedding and while she was pregnant with their fourth child Theodore, who was born just three weeks later.

The former Dancing on Ice contestant said he was throwing himself into work after the split, admitting: “It is gruelling, but it’s such a buzz.

"You don’t have a life; you just get on with the work.”

After finding fame as Luke Morgan in Hollyoaks, a role he will return to later this year, Gary won a host of female admirers for his raunchy role in Footballers’ Wives.

Fans have been calling for the X-rated show to make a comeback – and Gary gave the strongest hint yet they will get their wish.

He said: “Ask [the show’s writer] Brian Park.

"If I say anything he may not put me in it, so I have to keep my mouth shut, I think.

“He’s the main boss. Next year it would be lovely to do something.
