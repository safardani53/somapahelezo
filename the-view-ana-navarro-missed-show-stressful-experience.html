The View's Ana Navarro misses talk show after revealing extremely stressful experience

ANA Navarro has left fans confused after she missed The View on Friday without any warning.

The 51-year-old has revealed she went through a “stressful” experience just one day before her usual appearance on the morning show.

In the latest broadcast of The View, only four hosts say at the table – Joy Behar, Sara Haines, Sunny Hostin, and Alyssa Farah Griffin.

Joy, who fills in as moderator for Whoopi Goldberg on Fridays, did not reveal why Ana was absent from the table, leading fans to head to social media to as for answers.

One viewer simply pointed out: “No Ana today!”

Another tagged the show, as well as Joy, to ask: “Where is @ananavarro today?”

A third posted a confused thinking face emoji as they asked the same question but with all caps and multiple punctuation marks to show how badly they wanted a response.

While Ana hasn’t yet explained her absence, a recent Instagram post of hers might’ve contained a clue. 

On Thursday, the host revealed she’d just gone through an incredibly “stressful” experience.

She gave a big smile in a selfie alongside a new woman she’d just met in Florida.

In the caption, though, Ana revealed she was only able to smile now that the difficult day she’d been having had gotten better.

“There are still good, honest people in this world,” the 51-year-old wrote. “Yesterday, I lost my wallet, with my whole life inside it- credit cards, license, global entry, insurance cards.”

She added: “It’s been a stressful 24 hours. This kind woman, Jocelyn Sanchez, found it and took the time to find me and return it to me. I’m on Cloud 9!”

When Ana was on the show last Friday, she found herself getting irritated with one of her co-hosts, Sunny.

During Joy’s Banned Books segment, Sunny cut off Ana while she was speaking, in order to tell her something she said she’s tired of hearing.

Ana began: “Listen, the problem is that politicians, elected officials, like in my state of Florida, which has gone bananas–“

Before she could get to the main part of what she wanted to say on the topic, Sunny jumped in with a blunt: “That’s why you should move.”

Looking exasperated, Ana snapped back: “I’m not gonna move. You tell me that all the time and it irritates me! You don’t move, you fight.”

Trying to get the discussion back on track before the two could argue further, Joy asked Ana to finish her original thought on the topic.

Last month, it was during a discussion about a movie being banned in Ana's state of Florida that led to Sunny making the same suggestion.

Ana said, "This has gotten ridiculous," as her co-host Sunny blurted out: "You gotta move."

It took a few seconds for Ana to react to Sunny's comment, but when she did, she seemed frustrated.

"I'm not gonna move!" she screeched, before adding: "I'm gonna stay in Florida and I'm gonna fight from there."

Ana continued ranting: "So don't tell me to move again! People write me and tell me to move..."

Attempting to explain her outburst, Ana turned to Joy and said: "No, because they tell me that, they tell me to move, I'm not moving!"
