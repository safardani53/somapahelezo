BUTT Naked for LOVE Magazine!

As previously reported and depicted, there’s a whole lot of Kim Kardashian nude in the upcoming pages of LOVE.

The reality star is once again baring it all for a random publication, this time stripping down for the Spring/Summer edition of this British fashion magazine.

We’re talking COMPLETELY NAKED, folks.

And we’re talking it again via a couple of new photos that just hit Twitter, both of which will be included in the issue that goes on sale February 9.

Click away and you’ll understand why these needed to be blurred out for our younger readers…

“Can’t wait for u to see 30+ page spread,” Kim wrote of the LOVE pictorial a few weeks ago. “We shot for 3 days straight at a motel in LA. One night til 4am.”

Kardashian added that she was made up in “all vintage and custom Prada” for the shoot… although she failed to mention just how little of her body would actually be covered by this designer.

The LOVE issue will also feature half-sister Kylie Jenner (in a blonde wig) and half-sister Kendall Jenner, the latter of whom is only 19 years old and yet will be totally topless in many of her photos.

What can she say? She’s related to Kim Kardashian. She’s learned from the best worst.
