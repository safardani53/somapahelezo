'Definitely the nicest leggings around' shoppers claim as fashionistas race to nab the viral 25 M&amp;

FASHION fans are racing to get their hands on the viral leather leggings from M&S.

So if your wardrobe is in need of an upgrade, you’ll need to listen up - and get down to your local M&S as quickly as you can.

Fashion fan Hayley Laura Jones was left stunned with her new high-street purchase - a pair of faux leather leggings.

For just £25, Hayley was thrilled with her buy, which she claimed ‘fit perfectly’ and can be worn in ‘so many different ways’. 

Showing off her new leggings on social media, the blonde beauty wrote: “Love the fit of these! Trying on the viral M&S leather leggings.”

She then said: “OK, I'm gonna try these on now. I've not tried them on yet, so these are my first impressions.

“Material - I really like it. It's not too thick. It feels quite nice. The inside has got a bit of a lining, it’s quite soft.

“I went for a size 8 regular length. Usually in Zara leather leggings I go for a medium or a 10. 

“Sometimes I can get away with a small, but the last time I tried that, I was very quickly humbled and before I left the house I split them. So hopefully these fit okay.”

Moments later, Hayley modelled her new leggings, which she paired with a long-sleeved black crop top, as she explained: “They were actually really easy to get on. They have a lot of stretch to them actually.

“I was a bit worried at first but they went on really nice.

“These actually fit me perfectly. There is a little bit of room to move about and stuff, which is always good. I don't feel like I'm gonna split them.

“The only thing is the waistband did get a bit twisted when I was putting them on, but yeah, love them.

“I would say just stick with your normal size. I wouldn't size up because then I think there'd be too much material round the knees.

The TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @hayleylaurajones, has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly amassed 34,400 views.

Social media users were stunned at the leggings and one shopper even described them as the ‘nicest leggings around’. 

One person said: “I have these in the navy, they are deffo the nicest leggings around and keep their shape so well.”

Another added: “You look great.”

If you fancy getting yourself a pair of these £25 trousers, they are available to buy online or instore now.

Not only do they come in black - but there’s six other colours too, including pewter, silver, brown, dark burgundy, navy and dark green. 

They’re available in three different length options - short/petite, regular and long and sizes range from a 6 to a 22, so they are great for a range of body shapes.
