Actress Hits Home Run With Cameo

Lindsay Lohan didn’t just return to work … she CRUSHED IT.

The star, who recently signed on to guest star on Eastbound & Down‘s series finale as the adult version of Kenny Powers’ daughter, hit one out of the park.

Lohan reportedly "had a sober living coach with her the entire time she was on set," you know, just to make sure, but earned a standing ovation … seriously.

After filming, the cast and crew were "so impressed by the Mean Girls star’s performance, that they gave her a standing ovation," according to TMZ.

The HBO show’s co-creator Jody Hill even "hugged it out" with her.

Standing ovations … sober coaches … looking less like a walking cautionary tale … those visits from Ben Affleck and Oprah apparently worked wonders.
